Hello there, Traveler!
Welcome to the official website of That Which Exists, known as TWE. This site, TWEbsite, is home to all the information you need to get started on your journey.

This page is simply a welcome page, but feel free to use the box on the left side of your screen to navigate TWEbsite. Have fun!

Ruleset Page
This page is going to be the largest of the pages, by far, and contains details concerning the custom game rules in TWE. This is where you wanna go if you are looking for rules, how to play the game, combat information, formulas, or anything of the like.

Classes Page (Not Instantiated)
This page is a hub page that allows you to see all the available playable classes in TWE, and contains links to pages that explain those classes in detail. Come here if you aren't sure what kind of class you want to play!

Races Page
There are many options for what race your character will be in TWE, and this hub pages lists them all for you! It also contains links to pages that explain those races in detail. If you have another race you'd like to play, however, ask your Gamemaster!

Common Races
These are the most commonly seen races in That Which Exists. The vast majority of the people you meet on your adventures will be one of these races.

Uncommon Races
These races are few and far between, but can still occasionally be found in That Which Exists. Typically, these races are looked upon with suspicion in even the best of cases. Playing one of these races will affect how you are received by society.

Unique Races
These are the house specials, these races can only be found here in That Which Exists. They are awfully involved, but very unique to play. They will absolutely impact your game heavily if you choose them.

Lore Page (Not Instantiated)
This page is for those who love a good story. Here, the history of TWE is displayed. Whether you are looking for a map of the world, a history of its nations, or stories of past adventurers who walked the land, you will find something here you enjoy.
