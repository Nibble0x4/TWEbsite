Description
School: evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Effect: 15 ft Burst centered on Caster
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Victim's Choice




Aether Release
You shout, releasing your concentration to force a wave of aether out in all directions.

All creatures in the area must make a save of their choice against a DC calculated as if this was the highest level spell you can currently cast. On a failure, they take 1d6 points of force damage.

As a part of casting this spell, you may release concentration on as many of the spells you are currently concentrating on as you would like. Add 1d6 to the damage for every such spell released.

A successful save negates this damage.
