Description
School: evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft / 2 caster levels)
Targets: Up to Int modifier creatures
Duration: Int minutes or until discharged
Saving Throw: Will (Harmless)




Guidance, Communal
You extend your calming presence to your allies nearby, providing them a slight advantage on any one thing they attempt.

All targeted creatures gain a +1 competence bonus on a single attack roll, saving throw, or skill check. They must choose to use the bonus before making the roll to which it applies.
