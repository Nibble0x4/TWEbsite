Aetherial Conduit
You center your attention on your bonded item, summoning an extension of it and shaping it into an armament of your liking.

Weapon: You create a ranged or melee weapon you are proficient with. When you attack with this weapon, you add your Intelligence modifier to attack and damage rolls in place of your strength or dexterity.
In addition, you may choose a 1 cost enchantment to apply to the weapon. A list of enchantments can be found Here.

Shield: You create a shimmering shield of aetheric energy that floats by your side. It provides a shield bonus to your ac equal to your Intelligence modifier. This shield does not interfere with your ability to cast spells.
