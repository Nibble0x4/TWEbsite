Description
School: evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft / 2 caster levels)
Targets: 1 creature
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None




Aether Ray
You point to a single creature within range, sending forth a bolt of aetheric force.

Make a ranged touch attack against the target, using your Intelligence modifier in place of your Dexterity modifier. On a hit, deal 1d4 force damage.

Add a bonus to damage equal to your caster level.

Add a penalty to damage equal to the number of spells you are currently concentrating on.
