Description
School: Enchantment
Tier: 0
Components: Material
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Personal
Targets: Self
Duration: 1 round
Saving Throw: None




Focused Strikes
You focus your mind on your weapon, filling it with aether such that it assists you in your strikes.

All attacks you make with the weapon gain a bonus to attack rolls equal to your caster level.

Add a penalty to attack rolls equal to the number of spells you are currently concentrating on.
