Description
School: evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Effect: 60 ft Line
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Victim's Choice




Aether Torrent
You extend your arm and your will, releasing a concentrated blast of aether at your foes.

All creatures in the area must make a save of their choice against a DC calculated as if this was the highest level spell you can currently cast. On a failure, they take 2 points of force damage per caster level. Reduce the damage dealt by this spell by the number of spells you are currently concentrating on. A successful save negates this damage.
