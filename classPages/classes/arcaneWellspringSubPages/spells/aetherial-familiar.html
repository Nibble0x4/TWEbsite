Description
School: Conjuration (Summoning)
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 10 feet
Duration: Concentration
Saving Throw: None




Aetherial Familiar
You call out to your familiar, summoning an aetherial copy that is better prepared for battle and is more dispensable.

The aetherial creature possesses the following stat block:

Aetherial Familiar
HP: Master's caster level * Master's Int modifier
AC: Master's AC
Attacks: 1 Primary Attack + Master's Int modifier (1d4+Master's Int Modifier)
CMB: Matches Original Familiar
CMD: Matches Original Familiar
Speed: Matches Original Familiar



This creature moves on your turn and has 2 actions per turn. if you fall unconscious or stop concentrating on the spell, it vanishes.

If the aetherial familiar is killed, it vanishes and must be summoned again.

The Aetherial familiar functions as your familiar for all abilities and effects that care. The Aetherial Familiar cannot move more than 30 feet from you, or else it vanishes.
