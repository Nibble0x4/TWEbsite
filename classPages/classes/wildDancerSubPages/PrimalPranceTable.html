Table time!
This is a table available to the Wild Dancer through the use of their Primal Prance Advanced Step.

That is all... But, since you are here, I hope you have a great day! ~ Nibble

Table: Primal Prance


Result Flavor Summary Primal_Prance_Random_Effects_List
1 Show's Over! Lose all Verve. You cannot regain any Verve until the next day.
2-5 There's some room for Growth. Lose 4 Verve. You and your partner are transformed into plants.
6-10 Switch up the Roles! Lose 4 Verve. All creatures (including you) within 30 feet must succeed at a Will Save DC 20 or be randomly reassigned bodies.
11-15 I can fix him... Lose 3 Verve. You are fascinated by the beauty of an enemy, and may take no actions until they hurt you or until an adjacent ally spends 2 actions shaking sense into you.
16-20 LET ME FIX YOU! Lose 3 Verve. You are obsessed with beautifying the nearest allied creature. You cannot take any other actions aside from approaching and beautifying them until you have spent a total of 3 actions (1 on their hair, face, and outfit respectively)
21-30 Hope this works! Lose 2 verve. A random spell of a random level you know is performed by all creatures within 30 feet (Including you) with random targets.
31-40 OOP! Lose 1 Verve. trip and fall prone
41-50 I need my SPACE! Gain 1 Verve. You expel a pulse from both yourself and your partner. Each pulse deals 1d4 force damage per level to all creatures within 15 feet of the source and pushes them back 15 feet. A reflex save DC 20 negates the push effect and halves the damage.
51-55 A mile in my shoes! Gain 1 Verve. All surfaces within 60 feet of you become difficult terrain.
56-60 It goes with your outfit, I promise! Gain 1 Verve. All creatures in combat are given a silly hat of your choice. Any headwear they currently have is temporarily removed (even magic ones).
61-65 Fishy Fishy! Gain 1 Verve. Choose one creature you can see. If you perform the fishing reel dance, they will be magically pulled to any space adjacent to you.
66-70 Another One! Gain 1 Verve. You may cast any single spell you know as an immediate action without expending a spell slot.
71-80 Hit it! Gain 6 Verve. Music of your choice starts playing.
81-90 Places, Everyone! Gain 1 Verve. All creatures within your sight must succeed at a Will save DC 20 or have their locations scrambled.
91-95 Anything is possible! Gain 2 Verve. All surfaces within 60 feet becomes a Wild Nexus. All spells cast in this area must roll on the Primal Magic Events table.
96-99 And for my next trick... Gain 3 Verve. Add 30 feet to next turn's Dance Pool. Next turn, you are not restricted to only 1 of each kind of step you perform.
100 Now the Real Show Begins! Gain max Verve. You may take another turn directly after this turn is done.
