Description Lux is the Spirit of Light. She is ethereal, bold, and endlessly confident. Any adventurers trying to train her will need to ensure she feels properly transcendant.

Lux is a morning spirit, meaning her bond regenerates at the start of the day.




Core and Combat Stats



Core
Aggression [Agg]: 3
Resilience [Res]: 1
Collaboration [Col]: 5
Presence [Pre]: 6



Combat
Attack Roll: d20 + [Agg]
Assist Roll: d20 + [Col]
DC: 10 + [Pre]
CMB: d20 + [Agg]
AC: 10 + Res
DR: 2 * [Res]/Ghost Touch
CMD: 10 + [Agg] + [Res]
Saves: [Res]




Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Deal [Col] fire damage on attacks against shadow-affiliate enemies.
  2. Defense: Gain [Col] bonus on saves against shadow-based effects and AC against shadow-based attacks.
  3. Mobility: As a swift action, move up to half of your speed in an instant without provoking AoO.
  4. Refraction: When in the same place as a reflective surface, gain a copy reflection that mirrors your moves. Will save to know which is real without evidence.



Default Moves

  1. Dazzling Gleam
    • Type: Save to Negate (Fort)
    • Effect: Applies Dazzled [Pre] for 1 turn to all in 10 ft radius.
    • Range: 30 ft
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: This fails against creatures who cannot see it. Warnings also warn enemies.
  2. Glaring Appearance
    • Type: Save to Halve (Fort)
    • Effect: Deals [2 per Spirit lvl] fire damage to creatures who can see.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: This move can only be used as a part of Spirit Switch when she becomes active. This fails against creatures who cannot see it. Warnings also warn enemies.
  3. Light Screen
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Creates a straight wall of light 5 ft long per [Pre] that deals [Pre] fire damage to things that pass through. The wall fades when this cooldown ends.
    • Range: 40 ft
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: If the wall is destroyed or dispelled, the cooldown is set to 0.
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
