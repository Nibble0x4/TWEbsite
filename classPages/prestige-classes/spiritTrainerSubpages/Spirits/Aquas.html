Description Aquas is the Spirit of Water. He is smooth, flowing, and calm. He likes to relax and take it easy, so anyone trying to train him will need to work to keep him active.

Ignis is an evening spirit, meaning his bond regenerates at the end of the day.




Core and Combat Stats



Core
Aggression [Agg]: 2
Resilience [Res]: 5
Collaboration [Col]: 4
Presence [Pre]: 4



Combat
Attack Roll: d20 + [Agg]
Assist Roll: d20 + [Col]
DC: 10 + [Pre]
CMB: d20 + [Agg]
AC: 10 + Res
DR: 2 * [Res]/Ghost Touch
CMD: 10 + [Agg] + [Res]

Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Free Bullrush attempt allowed after one successful attack this turn.
  2. Defense: Gain [Col] bonus to CMD and DR/Slashing.
  3. Mobility: Breathe underwater, gain 50 swim speed.
  4. Breaking Tide: Deal [Res] extra damage on charge attacks. Walk on water with +20 movement speed.



Default Moves

  1. Crash
    • Type:Attack Roll
    • Effect: Deal 1d10 per [Spirit Level / 2] bludgeoning damage and push them 5 feet.
    • Range: 20 feet
    • Cooldown:2
    • Special: If the target is in watery difficult terrain, they are knocked prone.
  2. Wave
    • Type: Save to Halve (Fort)
    • Effect: 30 ft radius burst wave deals 1d10 per [Spirit Level / 2] bludgeoning damage and pushes all creatures back 10 feet.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 4
    • Special: If the target is in watery difficult terrain, they are knocked prone
  3. Flood
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Cover the ground within 20 ft radius with a foot of water that makes the terrain difficult for 2 rounds.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: Large creatures ignore it and Small creatures with a swim speed can swim in it.
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
