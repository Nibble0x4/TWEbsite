Description Tenebrus is the Spirit of Darkness. He is shy, reserved, and hesitant to show himself. Any adventurers looking to train him will need to bolster his confidence and push him out of his comfort zone.

Tenebrus is an evening spirit, meaning his bond regenerates at the end of the day.




Core and Combat Stats



Core
Aggression [Agg]: 1
Resilience [Res]: 3
Collaboration [Col]: 6
Presence [Pre]: 5



Combat
Attack Roll: d20 + [Agg]
Assist Roll: d20 + [Col]
DC: 10 + [Pre]
CMB: d20 + [Agg]
AC: 10 + Res
DR: 2 * [Res]/Ghost Touch
CMD: 10 + [Agg] + [Res]
Saves: [Res]




Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Add [Col] to attack rolls made while unobserved.
  2. Defense: Add [Col] to AC and saves while in low-light or darkness.
  3. Mobility: As a move action, move from one body of darkness to another within view.
  4. Fade to Black: Become Invisible in an area of low-light or darkness.



Default Moves

  1. Haunt
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Hide in the darkness. Opposed by perception.
    • Range: 20 ft
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: While hidden, add [Col] cold damage to any damage rolls made on an enemy within shadow and the spirit range of his trainer.
  2. Snuff
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Snuff out or cover all light sources in 100 ft and reduce light level by 1 stage within 30 ft for [Pre] rounds.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: This counts as a darkness effect and can cancel or be cancelled with light effects.
  3. Lurk
    • Type: Save to Negate (Will)
    • Effect: Single target becomes Shaken 1.
    • Range: 20 ft
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: This move can only be used as a part of Spirit Switch when he becomes passive. If the target does not see him become passive, it gains Shaken 2.
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
