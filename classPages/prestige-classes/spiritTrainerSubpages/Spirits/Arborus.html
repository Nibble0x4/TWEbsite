Description Arborus is the Spirit of the Forest and the Fey. She is whimsical, playful, soft-spoken and slightly mischievous. Anyone intending to train her will need to be prepared for her tricks and be ready to keep her entertained.

Arborus is a morning spirit, meaning her bond regenerates at the start of the day.

Default Moves

  1. Lash
    • Type: Attack Roll
    • Effect: Strike a single target and deal [1d4 per Spirit lvl] slashing or bludgeoning damage.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: If the surroundings are overgrown, roll CMB to grapple.
  2. Positive Pulse
    • Type: Save to Negate (Fort)
    • Effect: Heal [Pre] * [Spirit Lvl] health, or deal damage to undead.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: If a target has been affected by this today, it cannot be affected again today.
  3. Fae Glimmer
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Roll once on the Fae Event table.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: If in a place strong with Fey influence, roll twice.
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
