Description Ventus is the Spirit of Wind. She is swift and excitable, unable to remain still for long. Anyone hoping to train her needs to have patience and flexibility in equal measure.

Ventus is a morning spirit, meaning her bond regenerates at the start of the day.

Aggression [Agg]: 3

Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Gain [Col] damage electric shock attack once per round that can be released as part of any attack, spell, or as a ranged or melee touch attack.
  2. Defense: Gain Electric Resist [Col]. Enforce a 4*[Col] % miss chance on projectile attacks against you.
  3. Mobility: You do not provoke attacks of opportunity for moving, you gain a bonus equal to your lvl on attempts to escape grapple checks and bindings.
  4. Gone with the Wind: Levitate for up to [Col] rounds in a row.



Default Moves

  1. Gust
    • Type: Save to negate (Fort)
    • Effect: 20 ft Cone of winds push creatures 5 ft back per [Pre].
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 2
    • Special: Small and airborne creatures are pushed back further and larger creatures are pushed back less.
  2. Discharge
    • Type: Save to Halve (Ref or Fort)
    • Effect: Zap a creature to deal [1d8 per Spirit lvl] electric damage.
    • Range: 40 feet
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: If the target can conduct the electricity to another, they are affected as well with +2 bonus on the save.
  3. Swirl
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Winds within 20 feet support wind effects. Add [Col] to caster level, attack, damage, or DC of next wind effect or spell.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: None
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
