Description Ignis is the Spirit of Flame. He is quick, decisive, aggressive, and hungers for action. Any adventurers looking to train him will have their hands full keeping him in line.

Ignis is a morning spirit, meaning his bond regenerates at the start of the day.




Core and Combat Stats



Core
Aggression [Agg]: 5
Resilience [Res]: 2
Collaboration [Col]: 5
Presence [Pre]: 5



Combat
Attack Roll: d20 + [Agg]
Assist Roll: d20 + [Col]
DC: 10 + [Pre]
CMB: d20 + [Agg]
AC: 10 + Res
DR: 2 * [Res]/Ghost Touch
CMD: 10 + [Agg] + [Res]
Saves: [Res]




Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Add [Col] fire dmg to all melee attacks
  2. Defense: Half all damage from fire. Gain Fire Resist [Col].
  3. Mobility: Ride updrafts from large fires to launch 20 feet upward
  4. Fight fire with Fire: [Agg] bonus damage against creatures that are on fire or fire-affiliate.



Default Moves

  1. Incinerate
    • Type: Attack Roll
    • Effect: Attack single target in range. [1d6 per spirit lvl] fire damage if hit.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 2
    • Special: None
  2. Ignite
    • Type: Save to Negate (Ref)
    • Effect: Set single creature or object on fire. Suffer [Pre] burn damage.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: Extra Flammable Targets have -2 save penalty.
  3. Flamethrower
    • Type: Save to Halve (Ref)
    • Effect: 15 foot cone of fire. Deals fire damage [1d6 per spirit lvl].
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: None
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
