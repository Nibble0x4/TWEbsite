Description Animus is the Spirit of the Beasts, a chaotic and bloodthirsty being that craves the thrill of battle, the chase, and the hunt. He changes form constantly, but prefers the shape of predatory animals. Any who try to train him will find it dangerous for themselves and any other creatures nearby.

Animus is an evening spirit, meaning his bond regenerates at the end of the day.
