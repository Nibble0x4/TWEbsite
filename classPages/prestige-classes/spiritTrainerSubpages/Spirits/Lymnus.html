Description Lymnus is the Spirit of Order. It is a pure genderless construct of perfect order. It's only goal is to correct, align, manage, and set things the way it believes they ought to be. Any trainers trying to work with Lymnus will find it nearly impossible to get it to listen to them.

Lymnus is a morning spirit, meaning its bond regenerates at the start of the day.

Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Target who fails a save against your spell suffers half movement speed for 1 turn.
  2. Defense: Your form cannot be altered by another.
  3. Mobility: Your movement speed cannot be reduced by another.
  4. Special: Lymnus possesses your body and enforces order until Lymnus runs out of Special.



Default Moves

  1. Oppression
    • Type: Attack Roll
    • Effect: Deal [1d8 per Spirit lvl] force damage to single target and force them to roll concentration for any spells in the next turn.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 2
    • Special: If the target is Chaotic aligned, the damage dice are d10s instead of d8s.
  2. Patience
    • Type: Save to Negate (Will)
    • Effect: Enemies within 20 ft suffer [1d6 per Spirit lvl] force damage if they try to take actions outside of their turn.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: If the enemy is Chaotic aligned, the damage dice are d10s instead of d6s.
  3. Hierachy
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Trainer gives a number to each of their allies, excluding Lymnus. All numbered allies but the lowest number ally gain DR [Res]/-. When the lowest number ally is damaged, disappears, or is defeated, they lose their number.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 10
    • Special: When the highest number has taken damage, disappeared or been defeated, the cooldown is reduced to 1. Numbers disappear if Lymnus goes passive.
  4. Isolation
    • Type: Save Negates (Will)
    • Effect: Cut a creature off from external connections for 3 turns or until Lymnus becomes passive.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: The target cannot use or call on powers or entities outside themselves (EX: party, deity, patron, spirits).
