Description Glacies is the Spirit of Frost. She is cold, calm, and slow to communicate or befriend. She is a proud spirit with severe tendencies. Any who try to train her will find it a time-consuming process to gain her collaboration.

Glacies is an evening spirit, meaning her bond regenerates at the end of the day.

Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Gain [Col] bonus on DCs for spells with the cold subtype OR change a spell damage type to cold.
  2. Defense: Gain Cold Resist [Col] and become warded against environmental cold.
  3. Mobility: You cannot be made to slip and, on ice, may move after a charge up to the distance you moved prior without provoking AoO.
  4. Frostbite: Deal [Pre] bonus cold damage against a Slowed creature.
