Description Teras is the spirit of the earth. She is strong, steadfast, caring, and independent. Any adventurers trying to train her will need to be open to learning from her and working with her over longer periods.

Teras is a morning spirit, meaning her bond regenerates at the start of the day.




Core and Combat Stats



Core
Aggression [Agg]: 3
Resilience [Res]: 6
Collaboration [Col]: 2
Presence [Pre]: 4



Combat
Attack Roll: d20 + [Agg]
Assist Roll: d20 + [Col]
DC: 10 + [Pre]
CMB: d20 + [Agg]
AC: 10 + Res
DR: 2 * [Res]/Ghost Touch
CMD: 10 + [Agg] + [Res]
Saves: [Res]




Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Any weapon you wield gains the Crush Focus tag. If it already does, then deal [Col] bonus bludgeoning damage.
  2. Defense: Gain DR [Col]/Piercing.
  3. Mobility: Move straight through earth up to your speed during a move action, but you must end your move in the open.
  4. Earth's Embrace: Give your armor pieces the Thick tag while touching the earth. If they already do, increase their DR by [Res].



Default Moves

  1. Quake
    • Type: Save to Negate (Fort)
    • Effect: Shake the earth in 20 ft radius, dealing [Res] damage to structures and tripping creatures.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: If this Move breaks a large object, the Cooldown is 0.
  2. Rock Throw
    • Type:Attack Roll
    • Effect: Deal 1d12 per [Spirit Level / 2] bludgeoning damage.
    • Range: 30 feet
    • Cooldown: 2
    • Special: Add [Res] damage for every size category smaller than you the target is.
  3. Shell Armor
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Cover a willing ally with earthen armor that gives DR [Res]/Piercing.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: Lasts until it absorbs [Res] * [Spirit Level] or until a new Shell Armor is made.
  4. Assist
    • Type: Assist Roll
    • Effect: Support character action.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: The effects of this Move vary based on the assisted action.
