Description Eros is the Spirit of Chaos. They are pure chaos at their core, manifesting themselves as male or female on a whim. Their only goal is to undo and they delight in spontaneity. Any trainer trying to work with Eros will find the task nearly impossible due to their wild nature.

Eros is an evening spirit, meaning their bond regenerates at the end of the day.

Onboard Effects

  1. Attack: Increase critical multiplier of attacks by 1, but critical failures are more dangerous.
  2. Defense: When you make or fail a save, your position is randomly altered.
  3. Mobility: As an action, you can choose to teleport randomly within 60 ft.
  4. Special: Eros possesses their trainer's body and causes chaos until Eros runs out of Special.



Default Moves

  1. Erratic Strike
    • Type: Attack Roll
    • Effect: Deal [1d10 per Spirit lvl] damage of a random type to single creature (LINK TO random type table)
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 3
    • Special: Predict a damage type when you use this move. If you are correct, the damage dice is d12 instead of d10 and this move has 0 Cooldown.
  2. Entropy Beam
    • Type: Save to Negate (Reflex)
    • Effect: Deal [1d8 per Spirit lvl] negative energy damage in a 60 ft line.
    • Range: -
    • Cooldown: 4
    • Special: Any creature that fails to save against this must save against Scramble immediately.
  3. Gamble
    • Type: Support
    • Effect: Single Target flips a coin instead of rolling for next attack roll or save that comes up. If heads, crit success. If tails, crit fail.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 0
    • Special: None
  4. Scramble
    • Type: Save to Negate (Will)
    • Effect: Single creature is Confused for 3 rounds or until Eros becomes passive.
    • Range: Touch
    • Cooldown: 1
    • Special: If target gets the same Confused result twice in a row, roll again and take the new result.
