The Screech Rune


Description: The Screech rune hums with a pale white light, increasing with surrounding volume. Seeing it makes you suddenly aware of just how much sound you make.

Summary: Screech thrives on amplification. Sonic effects, music, or even other Screech runes work together to form a chorus of encouragement or pain.


Screech



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Air - The rune acts as a temporary volume enhancer, allowing whatever you say into it to be dramatically increased in volume such that someone a mile away could hear it. This rune lasts as long as you dedicate one hand and concentration to holding it.
Doing so forces a fortitude save for all creatures within 10 feet. Success halves 1d4 + Runetracer level sonic damage.



[2] Item: An item records and can emit a sound at a greatly enhanced volume such that it can be heard a mile away. This effect functions like the Air rune damage.
More RE can be spent to increase the damage dealt by the item's scream.

  • [4] - 1d6
  • [6] - 2d4
  • [8] - 2d6
  • [10] 3d4 and, on a failed save, the target is deafened for 10 minutes.
  • [12] - 3d6 and, on a failed save, the target is deafened for 10 minutes. If the save is critically failed, the deafness is permanent.



[3] Weapon: Grants the weapon the Thundering quality.



[2] Apparel: The apparel seems bolder, its colors difficult to ignore. The effects differ according to the slot the apparel fits:

  • Feet - The wearer's steps resound drastically louder than normal. This makes ignoring them hard and improves some performances.
  • Legs - The wearer cannot be moved by the Trip, Reposition, or Pull combat maneuvers.
  • Head - The wearer is immune to mind-affecting effects.
  • Neck The wearer's voice can dramatically change in volume on command, ranging from silent to loud enough to be heard a mile away. This does not deal damage.
  • Hands - The wearer's music made using their instruments that work with their hands dramatically increase in range, able to be heard a mile away. This does not deal damage.
  • Torso, Shoulders - The wearer gains resistance to sonic damage equal to 5*RE.
  • Waist - The wearer cannot be moved by the Bullrush combat maneuver.



[5] Surface: The region enclosed by the runes becomes highly sensitive to sound. Any sound made there is magnified 4 times, making stealth nearly impossible and making sonic effects generated there drastically more powerful.
Any creature in the region automatically fails its save against any sound-based effects.



Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • Any damage dealt by the modified rune is changed to sonic damage.


Additional Breaking Rules

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it releases a loud screech when it is destroyed.

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it can be temporarily disabled by any effect that silences sound.
