The Relocation Rune


Description: The Relocation rune glows with a prismatic light, but only when you are in motion. Observing it makes you restless, eager to move.

Summary: Relocation is all about movement. Weapons, people, armor, items, entire buildings, Relocation can see them all disappearing and reappearing at a moments notice.


Relocation



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Air - The tracer disappears and reappears anywhere within a radius equal to their 1/2 their movement speed in the area. (If they are underwater, this uses their swim speed.) If they do not have the appropriate speed, they must use 1/4 their walking speed.



[1] Item: Grants an item you can hold the Relocation-Ready status.

  • [10] - Grants an item you cannot hold the Relocation-Ready status.
  • [15] - Grants an item and any items inside it the Relocation Ready status.



[2] Weapon: Grants the weapon the ability to be teleported back to the wielder's hand as a swift action. In addition, the weapon can be switched out with the weapon wielded by a willing creature within 60 feet as an immediate action.



[1] Apparel: The apparel seems to shift slightly when it is looked at. The effects differ according to the slot the apparel fits:

  • Waist, Neck, Shoulders, Arms - Grants the wearer the Relocation-Ready status.
  • Hands - The wearer can, as an action, retrieve any item they are aware of within a mile radius that they can hold and that has the Relocation-Ready status.
  • Feet - The wearer gains a +5 foot bonus to their movement speed.
  • Head, Torso, Legs The wearer can, as an action, shift the apparel equipped in this slot to any willing creature within 60 feet. If they are already wearing something in that slot, then the effect is a trade. If the apparel does not fit, the trade magically reshapes the apparel to fit the new wearer until it is removed. Any magic items that require attunement do not attune to the new wearer.



[5] Surface: The area enclosed by these runes becomes a Runic Teleportation Circle. This circle can be activated by performing a 1-hour ritual.
Once activated, the Runic Teleportation Circle connects to any others of its kind created by the same tracer.
This circle can be used to teleport any creature or item that fits completely inside the circle and that bears the Relocation-Ready status. Doing so requires a 10-minute ritual.



Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • The modified rune can be moved to any other place within 60 feet if the tracer spends an action. The new location must fit the same medium as the original and doing this Burns RE equal to the rune cost.


Additional Breaking Rules

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it can be temporarily disabled by any entangling effect.
