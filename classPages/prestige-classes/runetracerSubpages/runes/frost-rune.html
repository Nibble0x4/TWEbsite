The Frost Rune


Description: The Frost rune glows faintly with pale blue light, pulsing softly in a manner that resembles light reflecting through icicles. The sight sends subtle shivers down the spine, warning even beasts of its power.

Summary: Frost can be used to conjure weapons or sheets of ice, infuse items with chill that can freeze liquids, and protect wearers from the bite of the cold.


Frost



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Can be used to form a fragile weapon patterned after one that the user is proficient with. The weapon is treated as a -1 enhancement bonus version of that weapon with the Fragile tag and lasts for as long as the rune is held. Doing so requires one dedicated hand and concentration. It can deal cold damage. More RE can be spent to increase the Enhancement Bonus of the weapon.

  • [2] - +0
  • [4] - +1
  • [6] - +2
  • [9] - +3
  • [13] - +4
  • [17] - +5



[1] Item: Infuses an item with cold, causing it to chill things when touched or broken, dealing 1d4-1 damage.

  • [3] - Causes the item to freeze liquids it comes in contact with. Deals 1d6 damage.



[1] Weapon: Causes the weapon to deal 1d4 bonus cold damage on hit.

  • [2] - 1d6
  • [4] - 2d4
  • [7] - 2d6
  • [10] - 3d4 and, on a critical hit, inflict Slowed on the target for 1 turn.
  • [12] - 3d6 and, on a critical hit, inflict Slowed on the target for 1 turn.



[1+] Apparel: The apparel takes on a soft, glittering quality and is infused with Cold Resistance based on what slot(s) it takes up and how many RE the tracer spent.

  • Hands, Feet, Neck - 1 * RE
  • Legs, Arms, Shoulders, Waist - 2 * RE
  • Head, Torso - 4 * RE
  • [4+] If at least 4 RE is spent, the following slots gain an extra effect:
    • Hands - Any mundane object grabbed is chilled or even frozen over
    • Feet - Surfaces beneath the feet are chilled or even frozen over.
    • Legs, Torso, Head - If the apparel is armor, it gains the Insulating tag.



[1+] Surface: A sheet of ice is formed connecting the runes. Any liquids in the area are chilled and potentially frozen.

  • [6+] - If at least 6 RE is spent, then all liquids are frozen. Any creature that is wet must make a fortitude save or is Slowed. All creatures in the area suffer REd4 damage each turn they spend in the area.


Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • The elemental affiliation of the modified rune becomes Cold.
  • Any constructs formed by the modified rune are now made of ice and gain all the properties of ice. They are immune to cold damage or damage from cold-affiliate sources.


Additional Breaking Rules

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it is susceptible to fire and can be melted by any fire effect that deals damage.

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it is immune to cold damage.
