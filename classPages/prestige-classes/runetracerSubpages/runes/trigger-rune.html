The Trigger Rune


Description: The Trigger rune awaits your input. Looking at it makes you restless, wanting to get things started immediately.

Summary: Trigger is an augmenting rune, one that is less important as a noun than it is as an adjective in most cases. It allows for more control over the activation times of runes.


Trigger



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Air - Any single trap, mechanism, or rune that the tracer can see within 30 feet is activated as though it were tripped or triggered. Tracing this rune takes 2 actions.



[2] Item: Empower the item with your runic signature, allowing anyone using the item to activate or deactivate any runes bearing your signature.



[1] Weapon: The weapon is warded such that, when interacted with in a way specified by the tracer, another designated magical effect in the vicinity is triggered. (Other effect must bear your signature or be modifiable by you.)



[3] Surface: The area enclosed by these runes is primed to trigger some other ready magical effect (such as a rune) when a creature enters the area. The conditions for this activation are up to the tracer and the trigger can be left as a simple mental note if she wishes.



Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • The modified rune will activate on a specific trigger chosen by the tracer at time of casting, instead of immediately upon being traced. The RE cost is still spent when the rune is traced.


Additional Breaking Rules

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it is treated as a trap for the purposes of disarming or disenchanting it.
