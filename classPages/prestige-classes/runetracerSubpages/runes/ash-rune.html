The Ash Rune


Description: The Ash rune is dark but somehow still glows a menacing dark red. It pulses erratically, flickering like flame. The lines seem to stick in your vision, even when you look away, burned into your retinas.

Summary: Ash combines the burning power of flame with dark, staining, blinding ash. It can burn or protect, blind or stain. Interacting with the Ash rune can be hard to recover from.


Ash



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Conjure a handful of smoldering ash that can be thrown as a ranged attack. It deals 1d4 + Runetracer level fire damage and, on a failed fortitude save, blinds the target until they spend an action clearing their eyes.



[1] Item: Ignites a flammable object until it is burned to ash. The flame deals 3d4 damage.

  • [3] - The flame of the object does not spread.
  • [5] - The object does not burn to ash.
  • [7] - The object does not have to be flammable. The flame deals 6d6 damage.



[1] Weapon: Causes the weapon to deal 1d4 bonus fire damage on hit.

  • [2] - 1d6
  • [4] - 2d4
  • [7] - 2d6
  • [10] - 3d4 and, on a critical hit, inflict Blinded on the target for 1 turn.
  • [12] - 3d6 and, on a critical hit, inflict Blinded on the target for 1 turn.



[1+] Apparel: The apparel becomes dark and ashen, stained with soot. It is infused with Fire Resistance based on what slot(s) it takes up and how many RE the tracer spent. Apparel worn in some slots gain special abilities.

  • Hands - The wearer can spread black, staining ash at will. This can be used to draw, trace, dye, or soil things.
  • Feet - The wearer can choose to burn their footprints into whatever surface they walk on, leaving difficult-to-remove markings.
  • Waist - The apparel smolders and flickers with flame, but does not burn up. This deals RE fire damage to creatures that grapple or are grappled by the wearer.
  • The apparel grants some fire resistance, depending on what slots they fill and how much RE was put into them:
    • Hands, Feet, Neck - 1 * RE
    • Legs, Arms, Shoulders, Waist - 2 * RE
    • Head, Torso - 4 * RE



[4] Surface: The region enclosed by the runes is filled with hot red and black volcanic ash. This effect extends upward from the region 30 feet. Visiblity within the region is reduced to 5 feet in any direction and every creature in the area takes REd4 damage each turn they spend in the area.



Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • The elemental affiliation of the modified rune becomes Fire.
  • Any constructs formed by the modified rune are now blackened and softly glowing with heat. They deal RE fire damage to creatures that touch them and are coated in staining soot and ash. They are immune to fire damage or damage from fire-affiliate sources.


Additional Breaking Rules

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it is susceptible to cold.

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it is immune to fire damage.
