The Barrier Rune


Description: The Barrier rune glows softly, without pulse or fluctuation in its light. It radiates strength, simulaneously daring you to try to approach it and warning you to stay away.

Summary: Barrier is all about protection and durability, reinforcing armor, keeping objects safe from harm, and constructing barriers to isolate areas.


Barrier



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Air - Creates a barrier around the user that reduces the next source of damage by an amount equal to twice the tracer's Runetracer level. Holding this requires the dedicated use of one hand and concentration. When damage is taken, the rune can be held if concentration is made against the damage taken.



[1] Item: Surrounds the item in a thin barrier, keeping its surface from being touched by anything that doesn't deal direct damage. The item can still be moved by touching its barrier.



[3] Weapon: Hardens the weapon, doubling its Hardness and HP.

  • [7] - The weapon cannot gain the broken condition as long as the rune persists.



[5] Apparel: The apparel gains wings or rings of feathers, as fitting the apparel. The effects differ according to the slot the apparel fits:

  • Feet - The wearer can ignore most painful, sticky, or slippery surfaces at will.
  • Legs - The wearer cannot be moved by the Trip, Reposition, or Pull combat maneuvers.
  • Head - The wearer is immune to mind-affecting effects.
  • Shoulders, Neck The wearer can no longer be beheaded, even by Vorpal effects.
  • Hands - The wearer cannot be disarmed.
  • Torso - The apparel grants a +4 armor bonus to AC, despite not counting as armor.
  • Waist - The wearer cannot be moved by the Bullrush combat maneuver.



[2] Surface: A barrier of force is erected along the borders of the runes. It extends upward from the runes 10 feet and then forms a box or dome, based on the user's choice. This barrier stops the passage of anything into or out of the area until the runes are destroyed or until the barrier's HP is reduced to 0.
The barrier has a Hardness equal to twice the tracer's Runetracer level. It's max HP is equal to 20 * the amount of RE you put into it.

  • [4] - Visibility through the barrier is reduced as if the light level was two levels lower (to a minimum of darkness)
  • [6] - Making contact with the barrier deals (RE/2)d4 points of force damage.
  • [8] - Double the HP of the surface runes holding the barrier. It gains RE hardness.
  • [9] - The barrier regains 2RE HP every turn at the beginning of your turn.


Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • This rune grants the modified rune Hardness equal to the RE imbued into it and increases its HP to four times that of a normal rune.
  • Any constructs formed by the modified rune have 4 times as much HP.


Additional Breaking Rules

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it has Hardness equal to the RE invested in it.

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it has four times as much HP as normal.
