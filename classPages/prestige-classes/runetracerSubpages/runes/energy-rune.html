The Energy Rune


Description: The Energy rune glows with a harsh, low yellow light that changes erratically. Looking at it causes the hair on your arms to tingle, as if they are preparing to stand upright.

Summary: Energy conjures the shocking power of electricity and the polarizing power of magnetism. It can protect wearers from shocks, force objects apart, and electrify entire areas.


Energy



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Fill your hand with elecricity, allowing you to deliver it as a touch attack or through a metal weapon and dealing 1d8 + Runetracer level electric damage.



[2] Item: Fill a metal object with static electricity that deals 1d6 electric damage to the next creature who touches it.

  • [4] - It becomes strongly magnetized. The object repels other poles strong enough to throw them up to 20 feet.
  • [6] - It becomes a powerful mono-pole. The tracer chooses South or North. The object repels other poles strong enough to throw them up to 60 feet.



[1] Weapon: Causes the weapon to deal 1d4 bonus electric damage on hit.

  • [2] - 1d6
  • [4] - 2d4
  • [7] - 2d6
  • [10] - 3d4
  • [15] - 3d6 and, on a critical hit, the target is Stunned for 1 turn.



[1+] Apparel: The apparel occasionally sparks and hums. It grants Electric Resistance based on what slot it fills:

  • Hands, Feet, Neck - 1 * RE
  • Legs, Arms, Shoulders, Waist - 2 * RE
  • Head, Torso - 4 * RE
  • [4+] If at least 4 RE is spent, the following slots gain an extra effect:
    • Waist - The wearer is cannot become Stunned or Paralyzed.
    • Hands - The wearer can make this apparel a weak mono-pole as a swift action.
    • Feet - The wearer can make this apparel magnetic as a swift action.
    • Legs, Torso, Head - If the apparel is armor, it gains the Grounding tag.



[4] Surface: The area enclosed by these runes is strongly charged. All magnetic objects are strongly repelled 5 feet from the surface.
A damaging electric field appears walling the space in, 10 feet high and forming a dome over the space. This field deals 2d6 + RE + fall damage to any creature that enters or exits the area.



Acceptable Usage

Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

  • The elemental affiliation of the modified rune becomes Electric.
  • Any constructs formed by the modified rune are now magnetic, allowing them to interact with metal and other magnets.


Additional Breaking Rules

Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it is susceptible to acid.

Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it is immune to electric damage.
