The Feather Rune


Description: The Feather rune seems almost soft, sitting lightly upon whatever surface it happens to be upon. You get the sensation that it may lift up and float away at any moment.

Summary: Feather is all about making things lighter, easier. It can reduce weight, grant small wind gusts, and carry you safely to ground.


Feather



Compatible Mediums

[0] Air: Causes the user to become as light as a feather, falling gently and peacefully as long as the rune is held. Doing so requires the dedication of one hand to holding the rune. This requires concentration.



[1] Item: Causes the item to become as light as a feather, prone to being blown about by wind. This works on objects up to 5 pounds.

  • [2] - 20 pounds.
  • [4] - 60 pounds.
  • [8] - 180 pounds.
  • [16] - 540 pounds.
  • [20] - 600 pounds.



[3] Weapon: Causes the weapon to sprout small wings. It can fly to its wielder's hand on command or be allowed to float on its own, following the wielder.

  • [9] - The weapon gains the Dancing quality.



[3] Apparel: The apparel gains wings or rings of feathers, as fitting the apparel. The effects differ according to the slot the apparel fits:

  • Hands - The wearer can generate small gusts of wind with their swings, whether they wield a weapon or not. The wind is strong enough to push an unattended feather-weight object 10 feet.
  • Feet - The wearer can move across any surface they could normally walk across without leaving tracks. Or, on command, the feathers can brush behind them to remove their tracks.
  • Legs - The wearer gains the ability to double their jump height and distance. In addition, they reduce the force of fall damage by 30 feet.
  • Head - The wearer becomes confused, losing the ability to recall much of their knowledge. This also wards others from reading the mind of the wearer.
  • Arms, Shoulders - The wearer gains the ability to glide on feathered wings.
  • Waist, Torso - The wearer is reduced to an eighth of their normal weight.
  • [16] - If 16 RE is spent, the following slots gain extra effects:
    • Torso, Arms, Shoulders - The apparel sprouts wings capable of flight with a fly speed of 60 feet.



[2] Surface: The air enclosed within the runes and 10 feet upward from them becomes thicker, reducing the impact speed of anything entering the area by 30 feet. If projectiles enter this area, their range is reduced by the same amount.

  • More RE can be spent to increase the reduction:
    • [4] - 60 feet
    • [6] - 90 feet
    • [8] - 120 feet
    • [10] - 150 feet
    • [12] - 180 feet
    • [14] - 210 feet
    • [16] - 240 feet
    • [18] - 270 feet
    • [20] - 300 feet


    • Acceptable Usage

    Noun: This rune can be used as a Noun, allowing it to function in the ways listed above. It can also be modified by other runes that are used as Adjectives.

    Adjectives: This rune can be used as an Adjective, allowing it to modify other runes used as a Noun.
    If used as an Adjective, the modified rune experiences the following changes:

    • All damage dealt by the modified rune becomes Nonlethal.
    • Any constructs formed by the modified rune weigh an eighth of their normal weight.


    Additional Breaking Rules

    Weakness: This rune and any rune modified by it is weak to bludgeoning damage.

    Strength: This rune and any rune modified by it is immune to nonlethal damage, electrical damage, and any wind effects.
