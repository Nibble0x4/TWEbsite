The Prestige Classes


Prestige Classes
These are classes that cannot be taken at level 1. They represent specialized adventurers with specific skillsets that must be grown into!
Before taking a level in one of these classes, the character must have several levels in another class and meet a special set of requirements unique to each Prestige Class.

Runetracer
A student of arcane magics from long ago, she calls upon power through volatile symbols called Runes.

Hoplite
A student of war dedicated to wielding large pole weapons to devastating and versatile effect.

Toxotai
A master of the bow, trained to use different vantage points throughout a battlefield.
