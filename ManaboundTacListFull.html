The Full ManaBound Tacs




Fire Tacs



Fire Punch- [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Strike your foes with flame balled in your fist.
If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 fire damage. If the attack is a crit or soft crit, then the target and any chosen creatures within 20 feet of the target are Inflamed 2. Creatures set aflame this way take 1d4 + 2 damage now and at the start of their turn until they dowse themselves
Rankup Effect: add +1 fire damage, and increase Inflamed by 2.


Explode- [ 2 Mana ]
Release an explosion of flame in all directions, searing all creatures within range, unless you restrain it.
All creatures within a 10-foot radius burst must make a reflex save or else take 2d6 + 1 fire damage. If you would like to exclude certain creatures within the range, then the damage is halved. Any creature who makes the save halves the damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 to the DC and 1d6 + 1 to the damage


Fire Blast- [ 3 Mana ]
Strike your foe and unleash a blast of flame that strikes at those nearby.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike. Regardless of whether the attack hits your initial target, compare the attack roll to the AC of the target and every other creature in a 20 foot cone. Anyone hit is dealt 1d6 + 1 fire damage. If any of these creatures are crit or soft crit by this Tac, then all creatures hit by the attack are Inflamed 2, taking damage now and at the start of their turn until they dowse themselves.
Rankup Effect: add +1 fire damage, and increase Inflamed by 2.


Fire Jet- [ 2 Mana ]
Strike and eject flame forcefully from the point of contact, burning your foe and launching yourself backward.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike, though there need not be a target. If you strike a foe, then you deal 1d6 + 1 fire damage. Regardless, you are launched in the opposite direction 15 feet. This Tac can be used to reduce fall damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 fire damage


Fireball- [ 5 Mana ]
Conjure a great 20 ft-radius orb of flame and hurl it through the air at your enemies.
Hurl the fireball at any spot within 45 feet. The moment it strikes something, it detonates and all creatures within 20 feet of it must make reflex save to halve 2d6 + 2 fire damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 to the DC and 1d6 + 2 fire damage


Burn- [ 2 Mana ]
Take a deep breath and imbue your strikes with flame.
For 1 turn, anyone struck by your fists is Inflamed 2, taking the burn damage now and at the start of their next turn. This Tac takes an action to cast, but can be sustained in the next turn and any consecutive turns as a free action by spending the appropriate Mana cost.
Rankup Effect: increase Inflamed by 2.


Firefoot- [ 1 Mana ]
Wreathe your feet in fire and run forth, scorching the ground behind you.
This Tac is cast as a part of a move action. Wherever you step for the duration of the move, the surface beneath you is set aflame for 1 round. Any creatures who step into this flame take 1d6 + 1 points of fire damage and are Inflamed 2, taking damage then and at the start of their turn until they dowse themselves. The fire can be bypassed by leaping over it with an acrobatics check with DC 14. You cannot run through another player’s space using this Tac unless you succeed on an Overrun maneuver.
Rankup Effect: increase Inflamed by 2 and the acrobatics DC by 1


Fire Shield- [ 2 Mana ]
Cast flame over a creature you can reach, wrapping them in warmth and protection.
For 1 round, the creature halves damage from fire and cold attacks made against them, and deals 2 fire damage whenever they make contact with another creature. This Tac takes an action to cast, but can be maintained each round
Rankup Effect: Increase fire damage by 1


Bonfire- [ 6 Mana ]
Summon forth flame from beneath you, billowing up and around you to consume all adjacent foes.
All creatures adjacent to you must make a reflex save to halve 1d6 + 1 fire damage. This fire damage is multiplied by the number of adjacent creatures (so if you are surrounded by 8 creatures it becomes 8d6 + 8).
Rankup Effect: Increase DC by 1 and damage (before multiplier) by 1d6+1


Heat Aura- [ 2 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
Focus and extend the scorching heat of your flame in all directions, intensifying the heat of your surroundings for 1 round.
All creatures within 20 feet of you are weakened, giving them weakness to fire (or removing resistance, if it is there), halving the power of ice effects, and melting ice structures. This Tac can be cast as an action, or cast as a part of another fire Tac. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up



Water Tacs



Water Slap [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Smack a target with the force of a small wave.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 bludgeoning damage and the target is pushed 5 feet. If they are obstructed, they instead take an additional 1 bludgeoning damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 bludgeoning damage to the initial strike and the additional damage for being pushed into an obstruction.


Wave Kick- [ 2 Mana ]
Kick forward, sending a wave pushing forward, bulrushing creatures caught.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike. The wave moves 20 feet in the direction you kick, moving along the ground until it hits an obstruction or a lack of ground. Make a CMB check against the CMD of every creature the wave meets. If it is higher, then the wave pushes those creatures until the end of its movement. Any creature slammed into a wall or another creature takes 1d6 + 1 bludgeoning damage for each such slam.
Rankup Effect: Add 1 to the CMB and 1 to the damage, and 5 foot to the distance.


Dam The River- [ 1 Mana ]
Calm your breathing and focus on the water around you, priming it to move at a moment’s notice.
For 1 turn, any water Tac you cast pushes 10 extra feet and deals an additional 2 damage on hit and on each collision. This Tac takes an action to cast initially, but can be maintained as a free action each turn by spending the Mana cost.
Rankup Effect: increase the damage by 2


Geyser Stomp- [ 3 Mana ]
Stomp the earth, sending water below, then summon it back out with a mighty geyser.
Pick a space within reach for the geyser to come from. Any creature in this space must succeed at a reflex save to halve 1d6 + 2 bludgeoning damage. If they fail the save, they are launched up to 15 feet upward and fall back in their own square or any adjacent square of your choice, taking the appropriate fall damage. If there is a prone creature adjacent to you, you may choose to cast this as part of an unarmed strike against them. If you do, they get no save and this Tac’s damage is multiplied on a critical hit. If you use this Tac on yourself, you take no damage and may reduce the height of the geyser as you desire.
Rankup Effect: increase the DC by 1, the distance by 5 feet, and the damage by 2


Hydro Cannon- [ 4 Mana ]
Summon forth a tube of water, pressurizing it until it breaks loose and fires at your opponents.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike. If it hits, then they take 2d6 + 2 bludgeoning damage and you must make a CMB check to bulrush. If that is successful, they are launched 10 feet backwards. If they strike a creature or solid surface, Both creatures take that same damage again.
Rankup Effect: increase the distance by 10 feet and the damage by 2


Rough Tide- [ 1 Mana ]
Raise your arms and let the waters follow suit.
If you are on dry ground, the area within 30 feet of you becomes waterlogged and is considered difficult terrain for 1 round. If a body of water is within the area, it becomes violent and attempts to drown anyone small enough in its area. The Swim DC to avoid being held under the water is 14. This Tac takes an action to cast, but can be maintained as a free action next turn by spending the Mana cost.
Rankup Effect: increase the DC by 1


Ocean Mist- [ 1 Mana ]
Summon forth water from all around you, forming a mist in the area and drenching everything.
For 1 round, the area within 10 feet of you is covered in a wet mist. The mist does not obscure vision, but clings to creatures within its area. This enables invisible creatures to be seen and fires to be dowsed. Any creatures made of fire in the area take 1d6 + 1 damage.
Rankup Effect: increase the radius by 5 feet, and the damage by 1d6+1.


Prehensile Cohesion- [ 1 Mana ]
Focus water around your arms, using surface tension to form tubes of coagulated water that can flail about on your command.
This Tac takes an action to cast, and grants + 5 foot reach to unarmed attacks. In addition, All water Tacs cast as a part of an unarmed strike while you are under the effects of Prehensile Cohesion are treated as if they are 1 rank higher. This Tac can be maintained next round as a free action by spending the mana cost.
Rankup Effect: increase the reach bonus by 5 feet.


Cyclone Push- [ 3 Mana ]
Pull water around you in a ring, then expand it outward rapidly, slamming foes away.
This Tac takes an action to cast. All creatures within your reach must make a fortitude save or be pushed 10 feet. If they are pushed into an obstruction or a creature, they and the obstruction take damage equal to your unarmed strike + 2.
Rankup Effect: Increase the collision damage by 2, the push distance by 5, and the DC by 1


Ride the Waves- [ 0 Mana ]
Level your breathing and cause the water to do the same.
For 1 round, you and any creatures within 20 feet that you choose may stand upon the surface of any water as if it was solid ground. This Tac can be cast as an action or as a part of casting another water Tac an action to cast. This Tac affects creature’s interactions with all water, including your other water Tacs. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up



Earth Tacs



Earth Headbutt [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Encase your head in a shell of stone and slam your target, breaking the shell.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 bludgeoning damage and you gain +1 natural armor bonus for 1 round.
Rankup Effect: add +1 bludgeoning damage and +¼ to the ac boost


Earth Rumble [ 4 Mana ]
Strike the ground with as much force as you can muster, causing the very earth to ripple and shake outward from you.
All creatures within a 5 foot radius must make a Reflex or Fortitude save against this Tac’s DC or else take 1d10 + 2 damage and fall prone. Any creature who is already prone receives no save. If you are falling, you may cast this as you approach the ground. If you do, the radius and damage increases based on how far you fell. For every 10 feet you fell, increase the DC by 1, the radius by 5 feet, and the damage by 1d10. You take no fall damage when you use this Tac successfully.
Rankup Effect: add +1 to the DC and +2 Bludgeoning damage


Earth Grip [ 3 Mana ]
Force the earth to mold itself around a helpless body, sealing it in a shell of hardened stone.
The shell prevents the body from moving, and protects it from attacks. The shell has 10 HP and 8 + your charisma AC. You may choose to allow for small breathing holes in the shell if you wish. This Tac is cast as an action and lasts for 1 round, but can be maintained as a free action next turn by paying the mana cost.
Rankup Effect: add +1 to the AC and your charisma modifier to the HP.


Earth Armor [ 2 Mana ]
Coat your body in dirt, hardened to stone where it can.
For 1 round, you gain DR/Adamantine 1 and Energy Reduction 1. This lasts for 1 round, but can be maintained as a free action next turn by paying the mana cost.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DR/Adamantine and Energy Reduction by 1.


Create Rock [ 1 Mana ]
Pull minerals from the earth and form them into a hard rock in your hands.
This is a very simple Tac. You can cast this Tac as an action. For 1 round, this rock is infused with your unarmed strength. The rock can be thrown or slung as any other stone, but is treated as a weapon with a x3 crit modifier and a base damage equal to your unarmed strike damage + your strength modifier. You do not have to be the one to throw the rock in order for it to deal this damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the bludgeoning damage of the rock by +1.


Rock Smash [ 2 Mana ]
Take a chunk of stone in your hand and bring it down upon your opponent’s head.
This Tac can be cast as a part of an unarmed strike. This adds 1d4 + 2 to the strike. If you have a rock you formed using the Create Rock Tac, you may use its damage instead of 1d4. If the attack is a soft crit, then the crit multiplier is increased by 1. If the attack is a crit, then the crit multiplier is increased by 2.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 2.


Earth Rush [ 2 Mana ]
Gather the earth to yourself, reinforcing your movements with its strength.
This Tac is cast as part of a Charge Action. For the duration of the charge, you have armor with 10 HP. If an object or creature is in the way of your charge, your armor takes 5 points of damage. If the obstruction is a creature, you may make an Overrun maneuver against them. If you are successful, they take the 5 damage instead. While your armor holds, your charge cannot be stopped by anyone but you.
Rankup Effect: Increase Armor HP by 4 and damage by 2


Earth Bend [ 2 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
With a single shift of your body, you can shift the very earth beneath you.
As long as you are in contact with the earth, you may use this Tac to cause a 5 foot cube section of the earth within 30 feet to shift 5 feet in any direction, but not into an occupied space, with the surrounding earth reforming to support it. This Tac can be cast as an action, or as a part of casting any other Earth Tac. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up.



Wind Tacs



Wind Kick [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Wrap your foot with wind and jump kick your foe.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 bludgeoning damage and you may immediately move 5 feet in any direction as a free action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity.
Rankup Effect: add +1 bludgeoning damage and 5 feet to the free movement.


Wind Sprint [ 1 Mana ]
Call the winds to your side as you move, defying gravity.
This Tac is cast as a part of a move action, and it increases your movement speed by 30 feet. If you strike a foe with an unarmed strike this turn after using this Tac, add +1 bludgeoning damage to the first hit.
Rankup Effect: add 5 feet and 1 bludgeoning damage.


Tempest Strike [ 4 Mana ]
Bear down on your foe with the force of all the winds that have propelled you, striking them with all the force you can muster.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike. This Tac deals an additional 1d6 bludgeoning damage + 1 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet you have traveled this turn. You cannot use this Tac in conjunction with the Run action.
Rankup Effect: 1 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet you have traveled this turn.


Tornado Whirl [ 2 Mana ]
Spin the wind and yourself along with it, tossing your limbs through the air like a ragdoll.
Increase your reach by 5 feet and make an unarmed attack against every creature in range.
Rankup Effect: Increase your reach by 5 more feet.


Shoot the Breeze [ 2 Mana ]
Use the winds to close the distance between yourself and anyone foolish enough to throw something at you.
As an attack of opportunity, when they are missed by a ranged attack, you may cast this Tac to move up to your movement speed. This movement speed does not provoke attacks of opportunity. If your movement ends within reach of a creature, you may make an unarmed attack with +2 bludgeoning damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the bludgeoning damage by +2.


Wind Shear [ 2 Mana ]
Command the wind to crash down on a single creature within 30 feet of you that you choose.
That creature must make a fortitude save to halve 1d8 + 1 damage. If they fail the save, then they cannot take attacks of opportunity for this round.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC by 1 and the damage by 1.


Updraft [ 2 Mana ]
Gather wind low, then rush it upward at a foe within reach, attempting to blow them into the sky.
This Tac can be cast as an action that provokes an attack of opportunity. If the target fails a fortitude save, then they are launched upward up to 10 feet.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC by 1 and the launch distance by 5 feet.


Winds of Fortune [ 2 Mana ]
When you would be struck by a physical attack, you may cast this Tac as an immediate action.
If you do, you increase your ac by 1 and summon a gust of wind to move you up to your movement speed. This movement provokes attacks of opportunity.
Rankup Effect: Increase the AC bonus by 1.


Windy Veil [ 0 Mana ]
Wrap yourself with the winds, creating an invisible shield protecting you from the dangers that fly in the air.
This Tac tcan be cast as an action or as a part of another Wind Tac. While this veil is active, the winds give you cover offering a 50% chance to deflect light projectiles from you. (This is useful against arrows, daggers, stones, bullets, and other similar physical projectiles. Heavy projectiles such as cannonballs are unlikely to be impacted.) In addition, the winds cushion you so that you are not subjected to fall damage.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up.



Force Tacs



Force Palm [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Focus your mana and strike your foe directly with your palm.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike, but it is a touch attack. If the touch attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 force damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 force damage


Gravity Cloak- [ 1 Mana ]
Create and sustain a constant force to follow a creature you touch.
For 1 round, the creature can run or jump onto any solid surface it desires, as the force will reorient itself perpendicular to that surface. The creature can move on these surfaces as if it was the normal ground. In addition, the creature gains a +2 bonus to acrobatics for that turn. This Tac takes an action to cast.
Rankup Effect: Add +2 to the acrobatics bonus.


Force Spike- [ 4 Mana ]
Conjure force to take advantage of your opponent’s armor to crush them.
This Tac is cast as a part of an unarmed strike. If your strike hits, it deals force damage based on the weight class of its armor. +0 if no armor, +2 if light armor, +4 if medium armor, and +8 if heavy armor. Natural armor is considered to be light armor by default, though different creatures may have heavier natural armor.
Rankup Effect: Add +2 to the light armor bonus, +4 to the medium armor bonus, and +8 to the heavy armor bonus.


Force Shot- [ 3 Mana ]
Conjure a ball of force and sling it at a target you can see.
The orb will travel any path it must in order to reach its target, swerving around and phasing through obstacles until it reaches the target. When it arrives, unless the target has some sort of force-based protection, the attack automatically hits and deals 1d6 + 1 points of force damage.
Rankup Effect: Add +1 to the force damage.


Friendly Fling- [ 1 Mana ]
Summon force at the back of a creature you can reach, launching it in a direction of your choice.
The creature is flung up to 30 feet in that direction, slowing before it makes contact with any surface until the movement is complete, at which point they free fall.
Rankup Effect: Add 10 feet to the distance


Forced Float- [ 2 Mana ]
Surround your foe with force, pushing them free from gravity’s restraint for 1 turn.
This Tac can be cast as an action at any creature you can reach. If they fail at a reflex save, they are not affected by gravity for 1 turn, as if they are levitating. You cannot magically control where they go, but any force on them will impact where they go.
Rankup Effect: Add +1 to the DC


Force Shield- [ 2 Mana ]
Summon a shield of force to cling to a touched creature for 1 round.
This shield grants +1 shield bonus to AC and nullifies any force effects that hit automatically on that creature.
Rankup Effect: +1 to AC


Force Field- [ 3 Mana ]
Summon a wall of force for 1 round.
This wall permits nothing but light to pass through it, not even force effects or incorporeal creatures. Pick a space border within 5 feet of you. You create a square wall that is 5 x 5 feet in dimension. You can create it as a rectangle smaller than this, as long as the total wall fits within a 5 x 5 square.
The wall has AC 12 with 10 HP. The whole wall takes damage when attacked, regardless of where it was hit.
Rankup Effect: Increase the dimensions by 5 x 5 and increase the hp by 5.


Forceful Entry- [ 1 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
Temporarily infuse your own body with force, allowing your strikes to pass through objects not made of force as if they were not even there.
This Tac can be cast as a part of an unarmed strike or as an action, allowing one of your unarmed attacks to be a touch attack. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up.



Ice Tacs



Ice Chop [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Form small icicles on your hand and chop your foe.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 piercing damage + 1 cold damage and the target suffers -1 to CMB and CMD until the start of your next turn. These debuffs do stack.
Rankup Effect: add +1 cold damage add -1 to the CMB and CMD debuff.


Ice Them- [ 5 Mana ]
Conjure as much cold as you can and fill your opponent with it, shutting them down and subduing them immediately.
This Tac can be cast as an action. If there is a prone or helpless creature within reach, you may make a CMB grapple against them. If you succeed, they make a fortitude save or else fall unconscious and cease all internal processes. While they are like this, their bodies do not age, think, act, poisons and diseases are halted, and they are completely stiff. You may keep them like this as long as you spend 1 Mana each turn. At any time during this, you may choose to end their life by unfreezing them wrong. When you wish for them to wake, you may release them and they will return to waking in 1 minute.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC by 1.


Bone Chill- [ 2 Mana ]
Conjure cold to surround an enemy and chill them to their core, weakening them against your attacks.
This Tac can be cast as an action. Choose a target within 40 feet. If the target fails a fortitude save, their crit guard is reduced by 1 and they gain brittle bones, giving them Vulnerability 1 (Bludgeoning) for 1 round. You can maintain this effect next round by paying the mana cost.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC, crit guard reduction, and the Vulnerability (B) by 1.


Bitter Cold- [ 3 Mana ]
Focus the cold on yourself, sapping the warmth from the air nearby and channeling to keep some warm and freeze others who would strain themselves.
This Tac can be cast as an action or as part of casting another Ice Tac. For 1 round, you are surrounded by a 15 foot radius zone of cold. This can be maintained as a free action next turn by spending the mana cost. All who would enter or start their turn in this area must succeed at a fortitude save or else suffer 1 cold damage for every attack, spell, or step they take beyond 5 feet in one action. If a spellcaster fails the save, they cannot cast a spell in the area if that spell has Somatic components. For each time that a creature is reintroduced to the cold and for each time that Bitter Cold is maintained, they must roll a new save.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC and the damage by 1.


Coldtrops- [ 1 Mana ]
Manifest cold in the form of many small, razor sharp icicle caltrops and cast them around.
This Tac can be cast as an action, or as a part of casting another Ice Tac. You generate enough coldtrops to cover 4 squares. You may choose any squares within 30 feet to place them, provided they are not blocked. Occupied spaces do not count as blocked. Any creature who moves into or out of a space with coldtrops as a part of any movement that is more than 5 feet takes 1 point of cold damage and has their movement speed halved for 1 turn.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 1 and the number of squares you can fill by 1.


FrostBite- [ 4 Mana ]
Reach deep into your foe, pulling forth as much of their crucial inner warmth as you can and consuming it.
This Tac takes an action to cast, and requires a successful Steal maneuver to strike. If the maneuver hits, you deal 1d6 + 1 cold damage plus extra based on how many debuffs are currently on the opponent. If:
Halved movement- 1 more damage and 1 more point of healing
CMB or CMD reduction- 1 more damage and 1 more point of healing
Vulnerability- 1 more damage and 1 more point of healing
Crit Guard Reduced- 1 more damage and 1 more point of healing
Any other debuffs- 1 more damage and 1 more point of healing
Rankup Effect: Increase the base damage by 1d6+1 and the damage and healing for each of the debuff effects by 1.


Freezer Burn- [ 2 Mana ]
Grip your opponent for just a moment, searing their skin with cold before letting them go once more.
This Tac takes an action to cast and requires a successful grapple maneuver to strike. If the maneuver succeeds, you grab your enemy and deal 2 points of cold damage with your hands before letting them go. In the place you grabbed them, their skin has been burned. For 1 round, they have Vulnerability 1 (Cold). You store the heat from their skin, so the next Water Tac you cast within 1 minute gains Steam Power 1.
Rankup Effect: Increase the cold damage by 2, the Vulnerability (Cold) by 1, and the Steam Power by 1.


StalagmIce- [ 0 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
Conjure ice nearby, within 30 feet, causing a spire to jut up in any unoccupied square.
For 1 turn, this Ice spire exists as a solid surface with 10 ac and hp equal to half your current hp. Anyone who falls onto the tip of this spire takes extra fall damage as if they had fallen 30 extra feet. This Tac can be cast as an action or as part of another ice Tac. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up



Acid Tacs



Acid Jab [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Jab your foe, acid dripping from your hand.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 acid damage and reduces the ac of the target’s armor or natural armor or shield by 1 until the start of your next turn. If the target has no armor bonus, instead double the acid damage dealt by this attack.
Rankup Effect: add +1 acid damage and 1 to the ac reduction.


Toxic Deluge- [ 2 Mana ]
Conjure a glob of specialized acid and send it to cover an opponent nearby.
This Tac is cast as an action, and its effects last 1 turn. If the target fails a reflex save, their elemental resistances are reduced by 1. If they are immune to an element, they take 1 step backward instead. (If they heal from damage, they are immune. If they are immune, they halve. If they halve, they now have resistance equal to their level that can be reduced) This damage can stack, up to reducing the target’s resistance to 0.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC by 1, and the resistance reduction by 1.


Consuming Strike- [ 3 Mana ]
Spying an opening in your foe’s defenses, strike with acid primed to eat away at their exposed flesh.
This Tac can be cast as a part of an unarmed strike. If the target has no armor bonus, then deal an additional 2d6+2 acid damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 1d6+1.


Gear Hungry- [ 3 Mana ]
Conjure a dripping coat of acid in your hand, and reach out for an opponent’s weapon, tool, armor, or shield in an attempt to ruin it.
This Tac can be cast as a part of a Disarm maneuver. If it is successful, deal 1d4+1 damage to the target item. This damage bypasses the item’s hardness, and if it reduces its hp to half, then the item gains the broken condition. If it reduces the hp of the item to 0, then the item is destroyed and becomes useless.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 1 and add 1 to your CMB to disarm.


Toxic Cloud- [ 1 Mana ]
Take a deep breath and exhale, allowing poisonous gas to spill out of your mouth and form an orb that, when broken, fills the area with a purplish haze.
This Tac is cast as an action. Select a space with 10 feet and throw the ball of gas there. Until the start of your next turn, the area within 10 feet of this location is filled with poisonous gas. Any creature who enters the area or starts their turn there must make a fortitude save or else gain 1 sickened level. The sickness lasts until they have a chance to take a long rest, and stacks up to 1.
Rankup Effect: Increase the DC by 1 and the maximum stack level by 1.


Blob Body- [ 2 Mana ]
Cover your body with excess acid, hoping to burn anyone foolish enough to strike you.
This Tac can be cast as an action and lasts 1 round, though it can be maintained as a free action next turn by paying the mana cost. When an enemy strikes you and deals damage with a physical melee attack or touch attack, the acid splashes off a bit and deals 1 point of acid damage to anyone within 5 feet. If this Tac is active, Earth Armor cannot be active on you, lest it be dissolved.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 1.


Acid Spit- [ 3 Mana ]
Spit forth a glob of toxic sludge from your mouth at your opponent.
Depending on where it strikes them, they may suffer a variety of negative effects. Make a ranged touch attack at a target within 20 feet. If the attack hits, you deal 1d6+1 points of acid damage and roll a d4. If:
1- They are hit in the eyes and are blinded for 1 round.
2- They are hit in the mouth and cannot speak for 1 round.
3- They are hit in the arm and take -1 on attack rolls for 1 round.
4- They are hit in the body and have their crit guard reduced by 1 for 1 round.
Rankup Effect: Increase the acid damage, attack roll penalty, and crit guard reduction by 1.


Lasting Corrosion- [ 1 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
Intensify the acid dripping from your pores, causing its damage to last.
This Tac can be cast as an action or as a part of casting another Acid Tac. As long as this Tac is in effect, all AC and Resistance damage remains. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up.



Electric Tacs



Electric Elbow [ 1 Mana ] [ BASIC ]
Strike your foe with an elbow wreathed in electricity.
This Tac is cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 + 1 electric damage. If the target is below you and not in contact with ground, then it deals triple the electric damage.
Rankup Effect: add +1 electric damage.


Bolt Trip- [ 3 Mana ]
Build electricity in your limbs then reach out and touch a creature, filling them with charge and sending them plummeting to the ground.
Make a touch attack against the target. If they are airborne, they take 1d6+1 points of electric damage and immediately get pulled out of the sky down to the earth. For every 10 feet they fall, they take 1 electric damage, in addition to the regular fall damage that a creature such as they might take. If the creature is under the effects of feather fall or fly or other effects that remove fall damage, they take fall damage anyway.
Rankup Effect: Increase the initial electric damage by 1, and the electric damage for each 10 foot fall by 1.


Chain Attack- [ 2 Mana ]
Build a charge in your body as you strike a foe, then allow that charge to guide you toward the closest un-hit foe.
This Tac can be cast as part of an unarmed strike. If the attack hits, deal an additional 1 electric damage then move toward the closest creature within your movement speed and make another unarmed strike. Keep doing this until you miss or until there are no other creatures in range. You may choose to aim horribly in order to miss on purpose. For each consecutive foe you hit, deal +1 electric damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the bonus electric damage by 1


Defeating Discharge- [ 2 Mana ]
When knocking a foe unconscious, you use their body as a capacitor and pull energy from them.
You may cast this Tac as an immediate action as a part of an unarmed strike that defeats an opponent. You gain in your body 1d8+2 electric damage that you store and do not take. This damage is not released until you are struck by an enemy attack, at which point all your built up electricity is released at all creatures within a 10 foot radius, with a reflex save to halve the damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 2 and the reflex DC by 1.


Charged Flurry- [ 4 Mana ]
Face down a single foe and unleash a flurry of increasingly charged strikes intended to wipe them out, personally.
This Tac can be cast as an immediate action after a successful unarmed strike involving an Electric Tac struck an enemy. You may make an unarmed strike against this foe at your highest Base Attack Bonus with a +1 electric damage. If your attack hits, you may do so again with a -4 attack modifier and an additional +1 electric damage more than your last hit. You may continue to do this until you miss. A natural 1 causes you to strike yourself. You may not target any other creatures with these attacks, just your initial target.
Rankup Effect: Increase the stacking electric damage by 1.


Static Shock- [ 1 Mana ]
Charge an airborne target with electricity that is just waiting for an outlet.
You may cast this tac as an action that requires a successful touch attack or as a part of an unarmed strike. The next time the creature touches ground or any structure, creature or other conductive material that is connected to ground, it and any creatures in contact with it takes 2d6+2 points of electric damage.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 2.


Jarring Shock- [ 3 Mana ]
Turn a foe’s metallic protection into a trap of arcing electricity.
This Tac can be cast as a part of an unarmed strike. If the target is wearing metallic armor, deal 1d8+2 electric damage and, if they fail a fortitude save, they are Jolted for 1 turn.
Rankup Effect: Increase the damage by 2 and the DC by 1.


Electrostatic Excess- [ 1 Mana ] [ EXCLUSIVE ]
Release excess charge building up in your body as a result of an electric Tac, sending it another creature within reach.
When you strike with a successful Electric Tac, you can cast this Tac as an immediate action to channel additional electricity into any creature within reach or, if you are airborne, who is within reach of a space directly beneath you. If you do, they must make a reflex save to halve electric damage equal to the amount you just dealt to your target. This Tac can only be cast once per round.
Rankup Effect: This Tac cannot be ranked up.
