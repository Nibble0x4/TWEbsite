Regions of TWE


The land of That Which Exists is split into 4 equally sized quadrants known as Provinces. These four regions have, over the years, developed distinct cultures, peoples, languages, and even currencies.

This page acts as a hub for easy access to each of the 4 Provinces and the unofficial 5th Province, an island in the inner sea known as Chet'Tun. It also connects to pages for other regions of TWE discovered by players on their adventures.

Provinces


Enthan
The Western Province, the stalwart Enthan Empire.

Taria
The Southern Province, the diverse Tarian Alliance.

Areth
The Eastern Province, the proud Arethian Empire.

Gruss
The Northern Province, the lands of the fearsome Grussian Tribes.

Islands


Chet'Tun
The island in the Inner Sea, home of the self-proclaimed Masters of the Sea.

Other Regions


The Archae
The enigmatic world beneath TWE, home to the most ancient histories and primeval creatures.

The Catacombs
The dark and mysterious tunnels that spread beneath the surface of TWE like a nervous system, ex-home to the Darker race.
