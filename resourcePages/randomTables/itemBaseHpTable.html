This is a table that shows the base HP of typical weapons and armor found in TWE. These values will be modified by factors such as: masterwork, enhancements, and materials.
The values here are the standard expected values for ordinary gear made of ordinary materials.
The HP for Masterwork items is double the values on this table. The total HP of an Enhanced item is the value of a Masterwork Item multiplied by the Enhancement Bonus.
Example: A Leather Vest(Light Armor) has 5 HP. A Masterwork Leather Vest has 10 HP. A +1 Masterwork Leather Vest has 10 HP. A +2 Masterwork Leather Vest has 20 HP.

I hope this isn't too confusing! ~ Nibble
