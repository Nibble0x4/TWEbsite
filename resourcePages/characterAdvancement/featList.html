Bond Feats
These are feats that can only be taken as a group when the GM allows it. They cannot be taken as a feat at any other time.
These feats are treated like Teamwork Feats for the purposes of being activated, but they do not interact with other abilities that refer to Teamwork Feats.
When a group is offered a Bond Feat, they all must choose the same feat.
When a group chooses a Bond Feat, each of the prerequisites must be met by at least 1 of the characters in the group.
These feats do not count against the number of feats a character can have.
Bond Feats are based on trust and experience, and may be lost if the bond they came through is damaged.
