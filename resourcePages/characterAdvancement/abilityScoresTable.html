Ability Scores!



Are you picking your ability scores for a new character? Then you are in the right place!
At level 1, every character is given 25 Score Points (SP) that they may choose to spend on buying Ability Scores. Any SP they do not spend is lost, so try and spend them all!
The table below shows the possible scores you can purchase along with their SP cost and the Modifier associated with the Score. (In game, the Modifier will be what is used for most things. It is very important!)

REMINDER: You have 6 scores to purchase: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. Be sure to spend wisely!

NOTE: All scores begin at 10 (the average for an adult). You may spend SP to raise them or regain SP by lowering them. Don't be afraid to have some high and low values!

IN ADDITION: The Ability Score bonuses and penalties from your race apply AFTER you have spent all your SP. This means you may begin play with values higher or lower than is visible on the table below.

Bonus Spells!
Spellcasters use Ability Scores or, specifically, one Ability Score to empower their magic. Each casting class has a different Score, whether Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma.
Each day, when the caster is preparing or refreshing their spells for the day, the caster gains bonus spells based on their associated Ability Score.
