Level Up!



Whether you are playing a character who just leveled up or starting play as a character above level 1, it is helpful to know the basics of what you get at each level!
Every class has a class table that defines what your character gets for leveling up. Those tables do not, however, account for everything. There are some things that your character gets for increasing their level regardless of what classes they have!
The table below shows the bonuses gained by a character at the specified Character Level. This is in addition to whatever they gain from their class at the respective level and, if the character is multi-classing, then their levels are added together to determine their proper Character Level.

Feat
At this level, you may select a feat for which you qualify.

Score Bonus
At this level, you may add a +1 bonus to any Ability Score of your choice!

Score Shift
At this level, you may choose to reduce any of your Ability Scores by 1 in order to boost another by 1!

Legendary Status
At 20th level, the character becomes a legend. What this means for power depends on the specific character and their story!
