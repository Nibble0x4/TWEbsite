Description
You have excelled in magical and mental learning, and are now ready to pass that on to others.

Prerequisites:
Instructor feat, INT 19 OR WIS 19 OR CHA 19

Benefit:
When teaching a spell, magic-related feat, mental skill, or mental ability score to another creature, gain a +4 bonus on the check and reduce the total number of Milestones needed by 1 (Min 1).
When teaching a spell to a caster of your same type, you may increase the maximum number of spells you can teach them by 1 of each spell tier.
When teaching a spell to anyone else, you may choose to teach them a tier 2 spell instead.
In addition, gain the ability to train a creature and grant them a +1 training bonus on a skill that you have a greater bonus in. You can grant this bonus a maximum of 1 time per skill.

Milestones needed = 2 (This is accounting for the -1 granted above)
