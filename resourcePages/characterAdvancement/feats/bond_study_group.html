Description
Your group listens and communicates well, learning as one.

Prerequisites:
Trainer Feat, Professor Feat

Benefit:
When an instructor is teaching something to the group, they may make progress toward milestones for all qualifying members who participate.
All participants becomes sore upon the completion of a milestone.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
