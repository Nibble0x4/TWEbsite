Description
You are as thick as thieves, and you have been briefed by the group healer on how to handle emergencies.

Prerequisites:
Heal +9

Benefit:
When using a Heal check to stabilize a dying ally, use the highest heal modifier out of the team that is trying to heal.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
