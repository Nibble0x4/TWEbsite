Description
Your group works well together, using some members to draw attention off of others.

Prerequisites:
CHA 15, Stealth +9

Benefit:
When a team member is attempting to distract someone peacefully while another team member attempts to use stealth, they may each take the higher natural roll between them.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
