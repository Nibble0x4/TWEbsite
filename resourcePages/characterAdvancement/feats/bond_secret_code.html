Description
Your group listens and communicates well, learning as one.

Prerequisites:
Thieves Cant or Linquistics 3 ranks

Benefit:
The team can use written, verbal, or sign language to communicate to each other. Others attempting to decipher the communication must make a Linguistics check with DC 20 or (10 + highest linguistics), whichever is higher.
If you are trying to use sign language covertly, you may also roll stealth opposed to the perception of those who would see it.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
