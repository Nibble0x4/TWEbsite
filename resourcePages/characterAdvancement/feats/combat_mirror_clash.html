Description
You have learned to see magical ray attacks as just another ranged attack to be deflected with the right tool.

Prerequisites:
Quick Clash feat, Deflecting Clash feat

Benefit:
During a Clashing stance, if you are wielding a weapon with reflective parts, you may spend an attack of opportunity to clash with a ray attack as if it was a melee weapon attack.
