Description
Your group has a single weapon that you all enjoy using, and you can pass it around in combat to great effect.

Prerequisites:
Weapon Focus(Chosen weapon), BAB +5

Benefit:
A single weapon for which someone on the team has the weapon focus feat is dubbed as 'communal.' The team can pass the communal weapon to any other member of the team within 15 feet as a free action.
Any member of the team who is not proficient with the weapon becomes proficient with it, but not all weapons like it. Any member who is proficient with the weapon can use the Focus ability of the weapon while wielding it.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
