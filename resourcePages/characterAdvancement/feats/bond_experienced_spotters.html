Description
Your group is aware of each others' limits, working together to keep everyone safe.

Prerequisites:
Trainer Feat, Professor Feat

Benefit:
Whenever a team member is about to fail a check and fall, drown, or something of a similar nature, you may roll twice and take the best result when attempting to intervene.
In addition, you may roll athletics to attempt to catch a falling team member, cutting their fall damage in half and taking the other half yourself.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
