Description
Your group has learned to take two directions while questioning, providing both a frightening and comforting approach.

Prerequisites:
Intimidate +9, Diplomacy +9

Benefit:
When performing a group interrogation, take the higher of the rolls between the two participants.
If intimidate is successful, the subject is cowed into silence.
If diplomacy is successful, the subject doesn't see the need to speak of the event to others.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
