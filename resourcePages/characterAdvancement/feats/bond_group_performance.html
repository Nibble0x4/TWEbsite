Description
Your group is ready to jump in and support their band leader.

Prerequisites:
Perform +9

Benefit:
When another group member is performing, if you have at least 1 rank in a perform skill, you may roll perform as well. The lead performer may choose the natural result of your roll instead of theirs.
If the performance is being done in combat, then using this feat to support them costs 2 actions.
If the performance is being done outside of combat, then add an additional +2 to the lead performer's result.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
