Description
One of you has trained the others on what to do in an ambush. You react quickly and trust each other.

Prerequisites:
Perception +9, Improved Initiative

Benefit:
When you perceive a threat before or during an action in a surprise round, immediately alert your team and end the surprise round.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
