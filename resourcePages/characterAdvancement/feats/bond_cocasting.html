Description
Your group listens and communicates well, learning as one.

Prerequisites:
Spellcasting, Spellcraft +9

Benefit:
If you are joining turns with an ally who is also casting a spell within 10 feet of you, you may each spend 3 actions to cast your spells together, mixing the magic into something new.
The spells you cast must be of the same level.
If the spells are of different elements or damage types, you may choose the higher damage and have the new spell deal damage of one or the other type, or split the damage in half for each type.
If the spells have the same damage type, you may add the damage together.
Choose the following for the new spell from among the options given by the combined spells:

  1. Range
  2. Area
  3. Targets


Saving Throw type (If at least one of the spells has a saving throw, the new spell must have a saving throw)
If the damage was chosen from one spell, choose an effect from the other spell to add onto the new spell.
If the damage is combined from the two spells, include all effects of both spells.
If there is no damage involved, choose one effect from each spell.
The DC of the spell is equal to the higher of the two DCs plus 2.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
