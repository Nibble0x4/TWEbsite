Description
Your group watches each others' backs and calls out to each other in times of need.

Prerequisites:
Evasion or Uncanny Dodge or Reflex +7

Benefit:
When a team member within reach fails a reflex save, you may choose to improve their result by 1 stage. If you are not subject to the effect, become subject.
If you are already subject to the effect, you cannot use this feat unless you succeeded on the save. If so, reduce your result by 1 stage.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
