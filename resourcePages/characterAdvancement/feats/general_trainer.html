Description
You have excelled at physical training, and you have what it takes to help others achieve what you have.

Prerequisites:
Instructor feat

Benefit:
When teaching a weapon proficiency, feat, physical ability score, or physical skill to another creature, gain a +4 bonus on the check and reduce the total number of Milestones needed by 1 (Min 1).
In addition, gain the ability to train a creature and grant them a +1 training bonus on a skill that you have a greater bonus in. You can grant this bonus a maximum of 1 time per skill.

Milestones needed = 2 (This is accounting for the -1 granted above)
