Description
You have trained extensively with a specific type of weapon, honing muscle memory and mastering the special quirks of the weapon.

Prerequisites:
Proficiency with the weapon you choose.

Benefit:
Select one weapon (Example: longsword). When making an attack with the weapon, you gain a +1 bonus on the attack roll. In addition, possessing this feat enables you to perform the Focus abilities of your weapon.

Special:
You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a new type of weapon.
