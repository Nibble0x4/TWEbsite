Description
When you strike as one, your weapon is deadlier in your grasp.

Prerequisites:
BAB +3, INT 15, We Strike Together

Benefit:
When making a simultaneous attack, add your weapon's base damage dice to your base damage again.
This functionally increases the base damage of your weapon before any multipliers are taken into account.

Special:
This is a Bond feat. It's effects can only be gained by those who gained it together as a group.
