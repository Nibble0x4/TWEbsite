Description
You adapt quickly to enemies who attack you multiple times.

Prerequisites:
Quick Clash feat, BAB +9

Benefit:
When you spend an attack of opportunity to clash with an opponent, you do not need to spend another attack of opportunity to clash with any other attacks made against you by the same opponent that turn.
