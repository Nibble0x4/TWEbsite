New Character!


It is time to welcome a new player character into That Which Exists!

Whether this is your first time or your tenth, the flow below should help guide you through the necessary steps to help you keep everything in order as you create your character!

The Flow


Step 1 - The World


This is an important step. Where in the world do you want your character to be from? TWE is an active, living world full of all kinds of people. Where do you see your character fitting in?


THIS LINK(not finished ): takes you to the Lore pages, the perfect place to familiarize yourself with the world of TWE!


Step 2 - History


Now that you know where you are from, what kind of person were you before you started adventuring?


THIS LINK takes you to the Histories page. Feel free to pick one from the list or speak to your GM about making a new one!


Step 3 - Race


What race are you? This choice will shape a lot of how your character experiences the world and the way the world sees your character.


THIS LINK takes you to the Races page. Pick whichever catches your eye, but be sure to run your selection by your GM to ensure it fits in the world!


Step 4 - Class


What sort of adventurer are you? A skilled warrior? A clever thief? A mighty mage? The choices are many!


THIS LINK takes you to the Classes Page. Have fun perusing!


Step 5 - Ability Scores


Your core 6 ability scores define what you are good and bad at!


THIS LINK takes you to the Ability Scores page. It will show you the ropes!


Step 6 - Gear


Now that you know who you are, it is time to gear up!


THIS LINK takes you to the Weapons page!
THIS LINK takes you to the Armor page!


Step 7 - Traits


This is an OPTIONAL step. You may choose a handful of Traits to quantify personality traits or quirks you imagine for your character. Use these as sort of character seeds that can inspire you to form a personality for your character beyond their race, class, and history.


THIS LINK(not finished ): takes you to the Trait Page!


Step 8 - Character Sheet


Nice work! Now it is just time to fill out your character sheet!


THIS LINK takes you to a PDF where you can build your character sheet!
THIS LINK(not finished ): takes you to an instructional page to help you fill out your character sheet quickly and effectively!
