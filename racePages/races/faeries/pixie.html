The Pixie Race


History
The history of pixies is as confusing as the creatures themselves. As fae who hail from the Other, their history in their own land is unknown. Here, however, they well known to be capricious, dangerous, immortal beings with a poor concept of mortality. They rarely fear or even seem to properly understand death, treating life-threatening situations as high-stakes games they don't wanna lose instead of the terrifying threats they are.
Mortals who run into pixies are often caught unaware, charmed by the 'cute' nature of the creatures and unaware of their magical power and cruel games until it is too late.

Culture
In TWE, Pixies act like vacationers. They are here to have a good time, almost always at the expense of mortals. They love to play games with death, strike curious deals, and delight in leading the big, stupid mortals astray. Though immortal, they can be killed more readily than they would like and tend to avoid more populated mortal encampments in favor of remote forests or places infused with strong magic.

Functionality
Pixies are highly vulnerable but highly potent magical beings, meaning they thrive in magical roles where they can stay out of direct danger. They make excellent wizards, witches, clerics, druids, bards, casters of any kind as long as they can avoid taking hits. They also do well as roles that try and stay out of sight, such as rogue.

Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to Pixies

Ability Scores
Pixies are weak and frail, but swift and magically potent.

+2 Dex, +4 to any single mental stat of your choice OR +2 to two mental stats of your choice, -4 Str, -4 Con

Base Speed
Pixies have a base movement speed of 15 feet.

They also have wings, giving them a 30 feet fly speed with perfect maneuverability. They struggle to fly at high elevations and must make Fly checks to avoid being tossed about by high winds. If they are wearing armor that reduces their movement speed, it applies twice to their fly speed.

Size
Pixies are Tiny creatures. This grants them a +2 size bonus to AC and attack rolls. It also gives them a -2 size penalty on CMB and CMD. They also gain a +8 size bonus on stealth checks and a -8 penalty on intimidate checks against larger creatures.

Itty-Bitty Armor
Armor pieces sized for Pixies have a 4 lower str/con requirement, but are limited to certain materials.

Pixie Magic

Intense Magic: Magic is so potent in Pixies that they gain a +1 bonus to their caster level and DCs of any spells they cast. In addition, if they cast a damage-dealing spell, they can increase the damage dealt by +1 per die rolled.

Whimsy: Pixies gain a +1 extra bonus on the DCs of any illusion spells or effects they cast.

Fae Orisons: Pixies may select 4 of the below Fae Orisons to know at first level. These orisons are cast using the same stat they chose for their racial bonus.
