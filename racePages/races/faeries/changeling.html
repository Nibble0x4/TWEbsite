The Changeling Race


History
Changelings hail from the Other, crossing over into TWE rather rarely and for entirely unknown reasons. No two changelings are alike, it would seem, and it is currently unknown just how many of them have entered TWE. Unlike other Fae, they often see no reason to sequester themselves away from mortal society and often incorporate themselves into the lives of mortals.
It is nearly impossible for the common man to tell a changeling apart from a true mortal, so it is impossible to tell how much of TWE history is actually shaped by these creatures.

Appearance
Changelings can take on any appearance they desire, so attempting to describe them here would be fruitless.

Culture
Changelings have no culture, as they are so rare and even more rarely encounter each other. Their goals are often to ingratiate themselves with society, sometimes losing themselves to the roles they choose to play. Some changelings choose to live a rather mundane life, enjoying the mortal life cycle before taking on a new identity to do it all again. Others work their way into more powerful roles, risking public scrutiny to attain goals they decide on for themselves.

Functionality
There is not a role in existence a changeling cannot play. They are the most adaptable race in TWE, capable of playing a stalwart warrior, sneaky assassin, powerful magic-wielder, or even multiple roles at once.

Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to Changelings

Ability Scores
The Changeling is nothing if not adaptable.

Gain a +2 bonus in an ability score of your choice.
You may change this choice once per day when you start the day.

Base Speed
Changelings have a base movement speed of 30 feet.

Size
Changelings are Medium creatures and thus gain no bonuses or penalties due to size.

Shifting Vitals
Changelings gain DR equal to their level against precision damage and critical hits.

Strange Physique
Changelings gain DR 1/- against physical attacks.

Shifting Favor
The Changeling may choose the Favored Class bonus of their favored class as if they were any other listed race, their choice per level.

Formless Changelings do not know what their original form looks like, since they changed instinctively into the spitting image of the first creature they ever met. They gain the following traits as a result:

  • The Changeling is genderless and cannot reproduce.
  • They can alter their appearance on command to take features from people they have seen in the last few minutes. They cannot change their size category in this way, but otherwise can change their size to match within the bounds of Medium or Small creatures. This changes their size, gender, and typing to match (They do not lose the Faerie type)
  • When the Changeling is attempting to impersonate a creature in this manner, they gain a +10 bonus on their disguise check.



Changeling Magic

Fae Orisons: Changelings gain access to all three of the below Fae Orisons at first level. Changelings do not suffer damage when changing into these forms, and they may make minor aesthetic changes to these forms whenever they enter them.

  • Familiar Form
  • Tricksy Form
  • Ouchie Form
