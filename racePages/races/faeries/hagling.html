Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to Haglings

Ability Scores
Haglings are pretty and convincing, but slender and frail.

+2 Cha and -2 Con

Base Speed
Haglings have a base movement speed of 30 feet.

Size
Haglings are Medium creatures and thus gain no bonuses or penalties due to size.

Gender
Haglings are exclusively female.

Age
Haglings are born in TWE, and thus do not have the 'Ageless' Faerie trait.

Half-Race
Haglings are always hag on their mother's side and half something else on their father's side.
They gain a Racial Pool with 2 points in it to spend on traits from their father's race, as if they were a half-and-half race.

Occultish Haglings may select one witch hex to learn at level one, casting it with her charisma instead of her intelligence, if she desires.

Hag-Whispers She gains a small stat boost and ability based on her mother's type.

  • Blood - As a sleight of hand, she may inflict Bleed 1 on a creature without their notice. With a drop of blood, she can alter her form to take on the appearance (not voice) of any woman. +1 Cha.
  • Rot - When dealing negative energy damage, She may add her Charisma to damage. She is harmed by positive energy and healed by negative energy. +1 Wis.
  • Plague - She is immune to disease, but can choose to carry it when she would be afflicted and afflict it on another by touch attack. +1 Dex.
  • Night - She is immune to sleep effects and can afflict sleep on living creatures who are otherwise immune. When using a Sleep effect that uses HD to decide who is impacted, double the HD amount. +1 Int.
  • Mute - She gains a +4 bonus on intimidate and Diplomacy checks if the target has not heard her speak. She gains Blindsight 10 feet. +1 Wis.
  • Sea - She can breathe underwater. +1 Str.
  • Green - She can mimic the voice of any woman she has heard. +1 Cha.
  • Winter - She may change the damage type of any elemental spell or weapon to cold. +1 Int.
  • Iron - She may lengthen and harden her nails into 2 claw attacks that deal 1d4+1 damage. +1 Str.
  • Ash - She may change the damage type of any elemental spell or weapon to fire. +1 Dex.



Hagling Magic

Fae Orisons: Haglings gain access to 2 of the below Fae Orisons at first level. These orisons are cast using their charisma.

  • Confusionate
  • Name Calling
  • Faelights
  • Faerie Ring
  • Look at This
  • Will O' Wisp
  • Displae



The Call
Haglings are susceptible to mental effects and fae effects, suffering a -2 penalty on saves against them.

As the hagling grows and matures, she will be unable to shake the voice of her mother whispering in her mind. This may manifest as a questline or special effects during play.

At any time while hearing the whispers of her mother, the Hagling may choose to answer the call and transform into a full hag. She becomes a monster but, if her bonds are strong, may not attack allies. She will leave the party.

If the hagling is called by a Hag while below 1 HP, then they are unable to resist the call and are transformed into a Hag of their mother's type.
