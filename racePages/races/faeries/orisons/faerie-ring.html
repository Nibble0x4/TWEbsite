Description
School: Conjuration
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal, Somatic, Material(Healthy Soil)
Casting Time: 10 minutes
Range: Touch
Targets: None
Duration: 24 years
Saving Throw: None




Faerie Ring
Cause local mushrooms to grow in a circle 5-feet in diameter, infused with fae magic.

This orison takes 10 minutes to cast. It conjures a ring of mushrooms that can connect to another Faerie Ring within 500 feet. Sitting in the circle for 1 round transports creatures to the other circle.

You can sustain a number of faerie rings equal to your character level. If you create a new one, you can choose to deactivate another.
