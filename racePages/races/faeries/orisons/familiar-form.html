Familiar Form
Transform into a special alternate form designed when you first learn this Orison, functionally becoming a magical Familiar.

Transform into your Familiar Form. Your Familiar Form is of your own design, based on a Tiny familiar of your choice. There are some rules for this design:

  • Your size must be Tiny.
  • Select an existing creature that can be a familiar. This determines your stats, the familiar bonus you grant your 'master', and the base of your appearance.
  • From that base, you may make visual alterations and may swap or add up to 4 features from other familiar type creatures.



In this form, you cannot attack or cast spells, but can offer to serve as a familiar for another creature. If they agree, you gain all the abilities of a Familiar of your level. If you are reduced to 0 HP in this form, you revert to your original form and are left unconscious at 1 HP.

When you transform, any gear you wear melts into your new form and becomes innert until you return to your normal form. Any auras or spells currently active on you when you transform persist.
