Description
School: Enchantment
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action / 1 Immediate Action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5/ 2 lvls)
Targets: 1 creature within range
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Will Negates




Name Calling
You begin a game with your target, one with fair rules but potentially disastrous results.

Requires Naming.

If a creature agrees to a game or says something that may suggest a game, then this can be cast as an immediate action. Target is locked into a Game with you.

You must explain the rules of this game to the target and it must have a fair, determined end condition. During the game, you may teleport to any unoccupied space within 30 feet of the target and become invisible to the target at will. If you directly harm the target, you may no longer become invisible to them. If a creature resists this cantrip, they cannot be targeted by it again for 1 minute.
