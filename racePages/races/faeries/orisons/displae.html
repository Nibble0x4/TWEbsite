Description
School: Enchantment
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal, Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Close (25 ft + 5 ft / 2 lvls)
Targets: All creatures in range.
Duration: 1 Round / See Text
Saving Throw: Will Negates




Displae
Call forth a fascinating orb of light that leads creatures astray.

Depending on the type of Perform you use, all humanoid creatures within 30 ft of you who can experience the performance must make a save or suffer a Compulsion for 1 round. If your Perform has multiple options, you must choose one when you learn this orison. This choice cannot normally be changed.

  • Footloose(Percussion, Dance, String, Keyboard) - The creature cannot resist moving their feet, and must either dance or expend an action to move at least 10 feet. They can choose to dance in their square, but doing so makes them flat-footed and provokes attacks of opportunity unless they also possess Perform(Dance). If they do, they may roll it to avoid these effects.
  • Emotional(Act, Oratory, String, Sing) - The creature is moved to tears, giving them a -1 penalty on attack rolls and a -4 penalty on saves against emotion effects.
  • Fired Up(Oratory, Wind, Percussion, Sing, Keyboard) - The creature is driven to grin with eagerness, gaining a +2 bonus on damage rolls.
  • Tickled(Comedy) - The creature is unable to resist laughing unexpectedly, suffering -1 action. Creatures who fail this effect cannot be impacted by it again for 3 rounds.



The DC to resist this orison is (10 + the caster's Perform bonus). If used in combat, the mechanical effects of this orison last for 1 round only. If used out of battle, the mechanical effects and duration do not apply.
