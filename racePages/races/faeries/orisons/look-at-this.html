Description
School: Enchantment
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal, Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Targets: 1 creature or object touched
Duration: 1 Round
Saving Throw: Will Negates (See Text)




Look at This
You draw a supernatural amount of attention to someone or something, making it difficult to look away.

All creatures, except for you, who can see the target must make a Will save or be distracted by it. Any creature that fails the save suffers a -2 penalty when performing an action that involves focusing on something else while the target is viewable.
