Ouchie Form
Transform into a dangerous enhanced form designed when you first learn this Orison, boosting some aspect of your being.

Transform into your Ouchie Form. Your Ouchie Form is of your own design, based on a your own base form. There are some rules for this design:

  • Your form must be a Small, Medium, or Large humanoid, or the same size as your base form.
  • UNLESS YOU ARE CHANGELING, this form must share the racial traits of your base form unless otherwise stated.
  • FOR CHANGELINGS: Pick one other race for your Ouchie Form and gain 3 points with which to pick from their abilities
  • You may make visual alterations to this form that do not apply mechanical differences.

While in this form, you gain a +2 bonus to 1 score of your choice. Once picked, this can't be changed. If Con is changed, gain HP equal to the max HP boost. At 5th level, 10th level, and 15th the bonus increases by +2

Entering this form deals 1 HP of nonlethal damage per level to the caster
