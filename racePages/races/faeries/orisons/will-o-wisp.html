Description
School: Conjuration
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal, Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Close (25 ft + 5 ft / 2 lvls)
Targets: None
Duration: 1 minute
Saving Throw: Will Negates




Will O' Wisp
Call forth a fascinating orb of light that leads creatures astray.

Conjure a single gleaming ball of light (or a cluster of smaller lights that remain within in a 5 foot space) within 30 feet. This ball has a 30 ft movement speed and can be directed by you as an action. If it passes adjacent to a creature, they must make a Will save. If they fail, they spend an AoO to take a 5 foot step toward the wisp. On their turn, they receive another save with a +1 bonus to the DC. If they fail again, they must use their movement to end their turn as close to the wisp as possible. If doing so would inflict direct danger on them (such as falling off a cliff, stepping into lava, or falling off a boat), they gain a +6 bonus on the save. Attacks of Opportunity are not considered to be direct danger. Each time they fail the save, the DC increases by 1 (To a maximum bonus of 1/2 caster level).

Once a creature succeeds on a save against a Will O' Wisp, they are not susceptible to another for 24 hours or until they forget about it.
