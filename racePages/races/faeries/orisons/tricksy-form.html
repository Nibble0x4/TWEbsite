Tricksy Form
Transform into a radically different form designed when you first learn this Orison, weakening yourself in order to gain new strengths and weaknesses.

Transform into your Tricksy Form. Your Tricksy Form is of your own design, based on a your own base form. There are some rules for this design:

  • Reallocate up to 4 of your ability scores, once picked this can't be changed. If Con is increased, gain HP equal to the max HP boost. If Con is decreased. reduce both current and max HP.
  • Your form must be a Small, Medium, or Large humanoid, or the same size as your base form.
  • UNLESS YOU ARE CHANGELING, this form must share the racial traits of your base form unless otherwise stated.
  • FOR CHANGELINGS: Pick one other race for your Ouchie Form and gain 3 points with which to pick from their abilities.
  • You may make visual alterations to this form that do not apply mechanical differences.

While in this form, you suffer a -2 bonus to all scores. This cannot drop a score to less than 1.

Entering this form deals 1 HP of nonlethal damage per level to the caster
