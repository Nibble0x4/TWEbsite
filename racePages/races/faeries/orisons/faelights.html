Description
School: Conjuration
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Medium (100ft. + 10/lvl)
Targets: 1 creature within range
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None




Faelights
Conjure a number of Faelights up to your Cha. These are softly glowing spheres of light that can move 60 feet per round.

They must remain within your line of sight. They may shed light as torches or not, your choice. Their shape is entirely up to you. You may choose to make them resemble someone or something else, though a good look at the faelight will reveal its true nature.

A creature only catching a brief glimpse of the faelight must make a Perception check (DC = 15 + your Cha) to identify its true nature.
