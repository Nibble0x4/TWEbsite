Description
School: Necromancy
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal, Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Targets: Creature touched
Duration: 10 Minutes
Saving Throw: None




Sprite's Blessing
You may touch a creature, imparting a bit of your spirit, and grant them a +1 divine bonus on saves. If you do, you suffer a -1 penalty on your own saves. You can choose to call your spirit back at any time. You can only grant 1 piece of your spirit to the same creature, but may grant this bonus to several creatures if you desire, increasing your penalty by 1 for each active blessing.

At 5th level, this bonus and penalty each increase to +2/-2, though you can choose to cast it at a lower level if you desire.
