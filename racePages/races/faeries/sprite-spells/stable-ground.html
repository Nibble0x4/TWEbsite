Description
School: Evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Targets: Creature touched
Duration: 1 Minute
Saving Throw: None




Stable Ground
You may touch a creature and cause the ground beneath it (If it matches your Attunement) to grow up and create a thin barrier protecting it. The creature ceases dying and cannot be moved unless the coating is broken. If it remains in the coating and is below 1 HP, then it regains a number of HP equal to your level each round until it stabilizes at 1 HP.
