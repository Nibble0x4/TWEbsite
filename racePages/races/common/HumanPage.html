The Human Race


History
Human history is the most prominent and well-documented history of all races in TWE. They live all over TWE, making their homes wherever possible, and enjoy writing things down almost as much as they enjoy keeping secrets. As a result, the world of TWE is full of countless secrets and mysteries waiting to be discovered. It would be fruitless to try to summarize human history here, so I won't even try.

Appearance
Humans have a great range of appearances, depending on who they are and where they come from. If you are reading this, then I imagine you've seen at least one human in your life for reference!

Culture
Defining Human culture in TWE is a difficult task, considering all the major civilizations were founded and inhabited by humans. Instead of going into the details, I will encourage anyone reading this to peruse the Lore pages of this site.

Functionality
Humans can fulfill any role imaginable, considering their varied nature and flexible skillsets. Combatant, support, magic, physical, melee, ranged, whatever you need to do, humans can do it.

Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to Human

Ability Scores
Human characters gain a +2 racial bonus to one ability score of their choice at creation to represent their varied nature.

Base Speed
Human have a base speed of 30 feet on land. They also have a swim speed of 30 feet.

Size
Human are Medium creatures and thus have no bonuses or penalties due to their size.

Type
Human are outsiders with the native subtype.

Defense Racial Traits

Bonus Feat: Humans select one extra feat at 1st level.
Bonus Skill Rank: Humans gain an additional skill rank at first level and one additional rank whenever they gain a level.
Skill Focus: Humans tend to choose something they really enjoy and pursue it with passion unseen by msot other races. At 1st level, they gain the Skill Focus feat for a skill of their choice.

Elemental Abilities
The Human are closely affiliated with water, being descended from water elementals. As a result, they have the innate power to manipulate water by expending energy from within themselves. Human have a Water Pool, a pool of points that regenerate after a full night's sleep and contain a maximum number of points equal to 3 + 1/2 their level. They can expend these points to cast from a list of spells at will. Each spell has a different cost associated with it, shown in parentheses.
