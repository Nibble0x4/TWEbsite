Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to Half-Orc

Ability Scores
Half-Orcs are reflective of the inherent abilities of both their human and Orcish heritage. As a result, they gain a bit from both.

+2 Str, +1 Con, -1 Dex, -1 Int, and a +1 bonus to any score of your choice.

Base Speed
Half-Orcs have a base speed of 30 feet on land.

Size
Half-Orcs are Medium creatures and thus have no bonuses or penalties due to their size.

Type
Half-Orcs are Humanoids with both the human and Orc subtypes.

Split Heritage Pool

Half-Orcs gain traits from both their human and Orcish parents. However, as there is great variety amongst humans and Orcs individually, it follows that half-Orcs have even more potential for diversification. Half-Orcs receive a pool of 4 points they can spend at level 1 to create a unique half-Orc that reflects the varied heritages of their parents. They can choose to spend these points on any combination of Human and Orc racial traits, as shown below.
Enchantment Resistance: Half-Orcs gain a +2 racial saving throw bonus against enchantment spells and effects.

Human Racial Traits

Humans come in several varieties. Humans, Enthan Humans, Trian Humans, Arethian Humans, Grussian Humans, and Kun'Achi Humans. Each of these variants has different abilities to offer depending on where the human was raised. Please see the corresponding pages for details.
When selecting abilities from Human Variants, you must follow the same rules for selecting abilities as that race does. EXAMPLE: A half-Orc cannot choose to take both an Imperial Combat Skill and a Regional Trait from Enthan Human. You also cannot take traits from two different sources of human, unless one of those sources is regular Human.

Orc Racial Traits

Orcs also come from different regions of TWE with a variety of abilities. These differences are not as pronounced as they are in humans, but they are still noteworthy. A Half-Orc can spend points from their Heritage Pool to gain any of the abilities listed below, but they cannot take abilities for different kinds of Orcs.
