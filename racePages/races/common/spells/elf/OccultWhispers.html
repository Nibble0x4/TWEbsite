Description
School: Divination
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 feet
Targets: 1 creature within range
Duration: 1 round or until dispelled.
Saving Throw: Will Negates




Occult Whispers
You whisper under your breath, projecting occult mutterings forth to settle into the ears and mind of your target.

The target must make a will save. If it fails, then a small voice whispers unknown words in their ear. The target suffers a -1d4 penalty on their next d20 roll this turn as they are distracted. A deafened creature cannot hear this voice and is immune to the effects of this spell.
If the penalty causes a creature to fail a roll, then the original caster gains a +1d4 bonus on perception, knowledge, and survival checks against them for 24 hours. This bonus does not stack.

If the target attempts to cast a spell, they must make a concentration check or lose the spell. If the target has any method of speaking into or implanting words into the minds of others, it can use that ability to duplicate the voice and spread it to others.

Once a creature's voices have given them a debuff on a d20 roll, the effect is dispelled from them.

You may not cast this cantrip multiple times in the same turn.
