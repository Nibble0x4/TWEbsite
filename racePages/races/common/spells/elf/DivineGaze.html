Description
School: Enchantment
Tier: 0
Components: Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 feet
Targets: 1 willing creature within range
Duration: 1 round or dispelled
Saving Throw: None




Divine Gaze
You cast your gaze upon a creature, surrounding them in a soft pulsing glow.

During the duration, the creature may choose to add 1d3 to one d20 they roll. This ability must be used prior to making the roll. If the roll is a natural 20, then this effect is not dispelled and instead lasts 1 extra round. If the result is a natural 1, the creature cannot be the target of this spell from the same source again for 24 hours.

You may not cast this cantrip multiple times in the same turn.
