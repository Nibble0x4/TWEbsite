Description
School: Evocation
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 10 feet
Targets: 1 creature or surface within range
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None




Primal Pulse
You extend your palm and your will alike, sending forth a pulse of force.

If the target of this spell is a creature, make a bullrush combat maneuver check using your intelligence instead of your strength for your CMB. On a successful maneuver, you push the creature 10 feet backward. If this movement would push them into a solid surface or creature, both the target and the surface or creature takes 1 point of bludgeoning damage.

If the target of this spell is a surface, compare your weight to the weight of the surface. If you are heavier, the surface is pushed 10 feet. If the surface is heavier, you are pushed 10 feet in the opposite direction.

You may not cast this cantrip multiple times in the same turn.
