Description
School: Conjuration
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic, Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 feet
Targets: 1 creature within range
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None




Arcane Missile
You scowl at a creature, forming a bolt of elemental force in your hands and flinging it at them.

Make a ranged touch attack with your intelligence in place of your dexterity. On a hit, you deal damage equal to your intelligence modifier.
The damage type of this attack depends on the element you chose when you first selected this spell.

You may not cast this cantrip multiple times in the same turn.

When the caster becomes 5th level, this cantrip gains an extra effect depending on the element chosen.

Fire: The target becomes ignites into fire on hit. This fire can be removed by an action spent patting it out and deals damage equal to the initial fire damage at the start of the user's turn.

Cold: The target is chilled, gaining Vulnerability to cold equal to the initial cold damage dealt until the start of the user's next turn.

Electricity: The target becomes a lightning rod. If a damaging electric area effect is within 15 feet of it, it is considered in the area and cannot be excluded by abilities.

Acid: The caster may choose one object on the target's body to also take the damage dealt by the cantrip.

Sonic: The target is deafened until the start of the caster's next turn.

Force: Increases to the target's AC from magical sources are ignored.

Positive Energy: If the target is undead or darkness-aligned, they take double damage from this cantrip.

Negative Energy: The target reduces healing gained by half until the start of the caster's next turn.
