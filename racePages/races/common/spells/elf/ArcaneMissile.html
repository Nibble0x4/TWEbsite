Description
School: Conjuration
Tier: 0
Components: Somatic, Verbal
Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 feet
Targets: 1 creature within range
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: None




Arcane Missile
You scowl at a creature, forming a bolt of elemental force in your hands and flinging it at them.

Make a ranged touch attack with your intelligence in place of your dexterity. On a hit, you deal damage equal to your intelligence modifier.
The damage type of this attack depends on the element you chose when you first selected this spell.

You may not cast this cantrip multiple times in the same turn.
