The Halfling Race


History
Halfling history among the people of TWE runs parallel to that of humans. Unlike in other worlds, halflings live about as long as humans do. They are a small, hearty, happy people who often finds themselves at the core of society, acting as the glue that holds the different races together, and also living their lives on the outskirts of cities where the simple farming life brings them great pleasure. You won't hear much about halflings in the history books, but that is because they are not often considered worthy subjects of writing. They are always there, smiling, bringing people together, and appreciating the ordinary things in life.

True Halflings trace their history back further than humans...much further. These beings are also referred to as Precedents, and they bear no blood relation to the halflings of today.

Appearance
Halflings rise to a humble height of 3 feet. They prefer to walk barefoot, leading the bottoms of their feet to become roughly calloused. Tufts of thick, curly hair warm the tops of their broad, tanned feet.
Their skin tends toward a rich cinnamon color and their hair toward light shades of brown. A halfling’s ears are pointed, but proportionately not much larger than those of a human. >br>Halflings prefer simple and modest clothing. Though willing and able to dress up if the situation demands it, their racial urge to remain quietly in the background makes them rather conservative dressers in most situations. Halfling entertainers, on the other hand, make their livings by drawing attention, and tend to go overboard with gaudy and flashy costumes. While elven clothing often plays off the beauty of the natural world, those elves who live in cities tend to bedeck themselves in the latest fashions. Where city-dwelling elves encounter other urbanites, the elves are often fashion trendsetters.

Culture
Halfling culture is very similar to human, except that it tends to slip away unnoticed, building the background and the 'ordinary.' Halflings are a common sight in smaller settlements, farming and dealing in the mundane but satisfying work of everyday life. In cities, they are easily overlooked both physically and socially as they tend to focus their time and energy into dealing with people. They rarely are at the forefront of innovation, learning, politics, or construction. Instead, they hold societies together with friendly faces, well-placed encouragement, and tightnit communities that watch out for each other.

Functionality
Halflings are small, meaning they are often weak, but they are quick and, depending on where they were raised, they can exhibit intuition and charm that lend to readily offering a helping hand.

Racial Traits
These are the basic traits natural to halflings

Community
Halflings are a people content to make up the framework of another's world. As a result, they exhibit a very practical set of skills that enable them to fit in sensibly in whatever community they were raised in. Depending on their background, a halfling gains different Ability Scores and two unique abilities.

Country Halfling
These halflings are raised in rural communities, often farming villages or the outskirts of larger settlements.

Ability Scores: They are quick and experienced, but are weak due to their small bodies. They gain +2 Dex, +2 Wis, -2 Str.

Content: They gain a +4 racial bonus on saves against [Charm] effects that attempt to make them desire or act toward something or someone.

Tell Me About It: They gain a +4 racial bonus on Diplomacy checks to acquire information from creatures they have successfully made friendly and comfortable.

City Halfling
These halflings are raised in urban communities, often learning to think and act quickly in the fast-paced city world.

Ability Scores: They are nimble and charming, but are weak due to their small bodies. They gain +2 Dex, +2 Cha, -2 Str.

Fast-Talker: They gain a +2 racial bonus on Bluff and Sense Motive checks.

Word on the Street: They gain the ability to use Knowledge (Local) to make gossip checks for any other Knowledge check in a place they are familiar with.

Coastal Halfling
These halflings are raised in fishing communities, living on rivers or lakes, or even the shore of the sea. They are similar to their country counterparts, but with more experience underwater than in conversation. They are often less talkative and less outgoing than other halflings.

Ability Scores: They are precise and intuitive, their muscles honed by the water, but their brusque nature tends to make them more abrasive. They gain +2 Dex, +2 Wis, -2 Cha.

Fishing Folk: They gain a +4 racial bonus to Survival checks to catch fish, track aquatic creatures, or lay aquatic traps. They are proficient with nets.

Grown by the Sea: They can hold their breath for twice as long as normal. In addition, they treat sandy difficult terrain as regular terrain.

Wild Halfling
These halflings are more rare, the result of being raised far from society. These are more familiar with how to track a beast or set a trap than they are with farming or chatting with neighbors.

Ability Scores: They are nimble and intuitive, their bodies hardened by the elements, but have little desire for book-learning. They gain +2 Dex, +2 Wis, -2 Int.

Fleet-Footed: Their movement speed is set to 30 feet instead of 20 feet like other halflings.

Soft-Stepper: They gain the ability to move up to half their speed without leaving any trail.

Base Speed
Halflings have a base speed of 20 feet on land.

Size
Halflings are Small creatures and thus gain a +1 size bonus to attack rolls and ac, a +4 size bonus to stealth, but suffer a -1 size penalty on CMB and CMD and a -2 size penalty on intimidate checks against larger creatures.

Type
Halflings are Humanoids with the halfling subtype.

Defense Racial Traits
Halfling Luck: Halflings receive a +1 racial bonus on all saving throws.

Skill Racial Traits

Hairy feet: Halflings receive a +3 bonus on stealth checks as long as they aren't wearing shoes.

Peaceful: Halflings receive a +2 racial bonus on Diplomacy checks to maintain or advance friendly relations or to convince someone NOT to do something.

Sure-Footed: Halflings receive a +2 racial bonus on Acrobatics and Climb checks.

Food Traits

Hearty Eater: After eating a meal, gain an amount of bonus hp equal to 1d4 + character level. This Bonus hp is lost first, does not stack, and is lost after 8 hours.

Food Lover: Halflings gain a bonus +2 on any checks related to food. This includes actions such as checking for poison, cooking, or convincing others to eat.
