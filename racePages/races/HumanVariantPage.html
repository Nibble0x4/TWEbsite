Human Variants in That Which Exists
In TWE, there are many different cultures and biomes. The different provinces and their various locales provide a varied background and environment for adaptble young humans to grow up in. Over time, this has led to the rise of variant humans, humans who exhibit more specific talents and traits that reflect their homeland.

This page shows the different variant human races that exist. Although there are many, there is no need for a human player from these places to use these races. Any human can always chosoe to be the regular human.

Each variant human has a set of Born Traits and a set of Raised Traits. It is up to the player which, if any, they choose to adopt. A human may be born in one place and raised in another, depending on their backstory and the GM's allowance.

Variant List
These are the most commonly seen races in That Which Exists. The vast majority of the people you meet on your adventures will be one of these races.

Enthan
These are the people of Enthan, the powerful western empire.

Tarian
These are the people of Taria, the diverse southern alliance.

Arethian
These are the people of Areth, the proud eastern empire.

Grussian
These are the people of Gruss, the wild tribal lands of the North.

Kun'Achi
These are the people of Chet'Tun, the independant island with a history of 100 years of civil war.
