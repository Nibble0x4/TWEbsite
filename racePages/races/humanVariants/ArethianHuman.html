Arethian Humans
Areth, the eastern empire of TWE, has long existed as a hostile and sequestered province. To the rest of the world, they are known as an egocentric, cutthroat, elitist society that jealously guards its borders and seeks to expand them at the slightest sign of weakness. However, Areth possesses a rich culture and history, once you get past their borders. They do not trade with the rest of the provinces, but inside of their own lands you will find a thriving economy that facilitates trade throughout all of their beautiful lands. They do not possess the varied climates of Enthan or the independant parts of Areth or Gruss, but their region is green, vibrant, and intensely magical. To the north lies an expansive forest filled with fae of all kinds, and to the south lies the Dark Wood, a dangerous thick forest and the home of many hags, witches, and other monstrous horrors. The people born and raised here feel as if they live in a separate world, a kind of idealistic land that fosters and cares for its own. Among all of this stands the Emperor, the ruler and protector of Areth, the blessed one, the incarnation of Xihe. The Emperor's power is absolute, and the flow of life centers on their capitol city of Yanor in the Lake. There is a massive difference between life in Yanor and the other big cities, and then life in the smaller towns of the empire. These differences manifest in the people who live there.

Each variant human has a set of Born Traits and a set of Raised Traits. It is up to the player which, if any, they choose to adopt. A human may be born in one place and raised in another, depending on their backstory and the GM's allowance.

Born Traits
These are the traits you gain for being born in Enthan.

Ability Score Improvements
The Arethian people are strong and proud, seeking excellence in both body and mind. They gain a +1 racial bonus to strength, intelligence, and a +1 racial bonus to a score of their choice.
This replaces the core human ability score modifications.

Armaments of the Empire
The Arethian Empire is renowned for their rejection of the ways of the other provinces. This rejection has led them to fashion outlandish weapons and dedicate themselves to their mastery. They gain the following feature:

Arethian Arms- You gain proficiency with two exotic weapons of your choice. If you gain proficiency in martial weapons from your class, you lose that proficiency and instead gain proficiency in all Exotic weapons. If you gain proficiency in all exotic weapons, then you gain the Weapon Focus feat for the two exotic weapons you chose.
This replaces the core human Bonus Feat trait.

Raised Traits
These are the traits you gain for being raised in Areth. You can pick one of the following, either a single Imperial Combat Style or a single Civilization Trait. Whether or not your character has a military background is likely to play a role here.
Whichever trait you pick, it replaces the core human Skilled trait.

Imperial Combat Styles:
The Arethian Empire prides itself in a varied offense and defense. Its recruits are trained to fulfill several different roles, depending on their aptitude. You may select one of the following options, regardless of where you are from in Areth.

Mounted Archery- Gain the Mounted Combat and Mounted Archery feats as bonus feats. You take no penalties on attack rolls for firing while riding.

Clandestine- If you successfully strike a target with an attack while they are unaware of your whereabouts, you may deal 1d8 extra damage and move up to your speed. If you reach cover, you may roll stealth to remain hidden.

Dueling- If your target has attacked in this combat but has only hit you, you gain a +4 bonus to melee attack and damage rolls, and a +2 bonus to AC against that target.

Pyrotechnic- Whenever you deal fire damage to a foe, you may choose to make it Splash Damage. Any creature targeted by the Splash damage (Other than the original target) may attempt a Reflex Save (DC 10 + 1/2 lvl + highest mental modifier) to negate the damage.

Weapon Specialist- Gain the Weapon Focus feat for 2 exotic weapons of your choice that you are proficient with. If you already have the Weapon Focus feat for that weapon, gain the Weapon Specialization feat instead.

Banzai- Gain the Power Attack feat as a bonus feat. In addition, whenever an effect would penalize your attack rolls, you may choose to take that penalty to your ac instead.

Civilization Traits:

Arethian citizens experience a very different life, depending on what part of the empire they were raised in.

Capitol Education- You have been given the chance to learn much that you otherwise wouldn't, surrounded by such a high concentration of varied and skilled individuals. Select 3 skills of your choice that are not class skills. They are now class skills.
City Homes that qualify: Yanor in the Lake

Urban Caution- You are raised to act carefully, always paying close attention to the things that others aren't saying. Gain a +3 bonus on Bluff, Disguise, and Sense Motive checks when in conversation with someone.
City Homes that qualify: Kyomana, Omeni, Ekesh, Arkei

Rural Initiative- You don't wait for things to get done, you do them. You are a hard worker, and that much is clear to all who meet you. Gain a +4 bonus on initiative checks, and you are an appealing option to any who are looking to hire someone for a job.
City Homes that qualify: Plota, Kyomana-Ka, Omeni-Ka, Ekesh-Ka, Arkei-Ka
